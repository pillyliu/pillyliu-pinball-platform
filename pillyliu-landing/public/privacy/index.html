Pinball App Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 9, 2026

Pinball App ("the App") is provided by Peter Liu ("I," "me," "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes and how it is used.

1. Information Collected

The App is designed to work without account sign-in and does not require you to provide personal information to use core features.

The App may process:

  • League and game content files downloaded from the app data source (standings, stats, targets, machine info, rulesheets, images)
  • Basic technical request data handled by your device/network stack when fetching remote content (for example, IP address at the network level)

No sensitive personal information is intentionally collected through in-app forms.

2. How Information Is Used

Data is used to:

  • Display league standings, stats, targets, and machine library content
  • Cache content locally for performance and offline-friendly behavior
  • Refresh content metadata and updates

3. Data Storage

The App stores downloaded content files in on-device cache storage to improve load speed and availability.

  • Cache data can be cleared by uninstalling/reinstalling the App
  • No in-app account system or cloud profile sync is provided at this time

4. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are used in the App
  • No cross-app tracking is used for targeted advertising
  • If this changes, this policy will be updated

5. Third-Party Content and Links

The App may display or link to third-party resources, including external websites and YouTube-hosted content.

When you open third-party content, their privacy policies and terms apply.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. Personal information is not knowingly collected from children.

7. Data Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Personal data is not shared for advertising purposes. Limited technical data may be processed by hosting/network providers as part of normal web delivery.

8. Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect content delivery and app operation. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Choices

  • Stop all data processing by uninstalling the App
  • Avoid opening third-party links if you do not want their services to process data

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
