<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Jungle Queen EM - Gottlieb | 1977</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/bf88fcf1-56a4-427b-be2e-ee208a353721-medium.jpg" alt="Jungle Queen backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> JQ</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">finish full banks of drop targets, then shoot either the side lanes from below, the top saucers, or UTAD off the edges of the bumpers to get the ball to fall into the now-lit side lanes from above.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumper slams and rebounds off of the drop targets. The feeds from the sides almost always contact a flipper, but you’ll need to determine how to handle that as each unit differs a bit, e.g. whether to tip it with the upper mini-flipper or let it go by. The saucer kickouts are usually safe.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The PAPA video on this is one of the better EM ones.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">First goal: shoot the drops for bonus and to light the side lanes for 5000 [all 5 drops on one side lights the lane behind them]. Use the upper stubby flippers to deflect the ball or shoot the lower drops but try not to use them too much. Transfer to lower flippers whenever possible.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">When the ball is coming fast towards the inside of a lower flipper, hold it up to try to get the ball to roll up the return lane into the A or C. When the ball is falling towards the center of a flipper, let it dead bounce. You want to complete A-B-C since that doubles your bonus. Bonus is automatically doubled on your last ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Once all of the drops on a side are down, shoot for the upper saucer above them for 5000. If the shot misses, it may still roll down the lit 5000 lane below. If the flippers are on the stronger side and you’re pretty accurate, you can shoot the side lanes from the bottom once lit, too, and sometimes get two 5000s via an up-and-down.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: kickouts from the two top saucers and roll downs on the right and left sides. Can you let top saucer kickouts dead bounce? Can you deflect side roll down feeds with the stub flippers to the opposite lower flipper? Which strategy you choose once your sides are lit for 5000 depends on both your accuracy and what the kickouts from the top saucers and the drops from the sides do. You want both a shot you can hit and a return you can get control of for whichever choice, top saucer or side lane, you pick.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">the “B” lane since you can get A and C below on the playfield.</p></section></div>
