<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Darling EM - Williams | 1973</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/4ec9cf28-c2ab-45d9-8793-a2cf1831be63-medium.jpg" alt="Darling backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 2</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via the side slingshots, ideally by going over either of the small rollover buttons.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">There’s no ball control to speak of, it just bounces around. The straighter up-and-down the ball falls from the top, the safer it tends to be - - it’s then more likely to come to a flipper or a saucer behind a flipper. Side to side action drains more, both center drains and outlanes. Shots at the looping captive balls risk center drains.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Darling is the 2-player version of Jubilee, with the same central captive ball horseshoe and side slingshot design.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">What to do? Build your bonus, keep the ball alive; the points in the game other than bonus are trivial. Yawn. Bonus is earned by the top lanes (two when lit), by looping a captive ball to the opposite side, by the two small rollover buttons next to the bottom of the captive ball enclosure and by the outlanes. Bonus goes up to 20K. Bonus is doubled on the last ball; double bonus cannot be earned on other balls.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Because the game is so unbalanced towards bonus, you rarely see it in competition; a player with a big lead is all but impossible to catch.</p></div></section></div>
