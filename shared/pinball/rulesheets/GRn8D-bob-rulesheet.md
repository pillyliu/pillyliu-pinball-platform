<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Super Soccer EM - Gottlieb | 1975</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/1243cd4a-4200-4354-b7bd-a01ee74a90f4-medium.jpg" alt="Super Soccer backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD - - through the spinner from the left flipper, and from the right flipper to the collect bonus saucer or, if the ball continues past that, the mystery add bonus saucer. Rinse and repeat.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Mild bias Right.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Heavily weighted towards balls exiting the bumpers. Rebounds off the two stand-up targets and any shot that hits a post, including the ones near the stand-up targets, are at high risk, as are non-grazing bumper hits.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">One of the few games that use a backglass-held plastic ball rail to track your accumulated bonus. In each case, bonus max is 10,000 [ten balls]. Like “300”, it has a mystery saucer that adds 1, 2 or 3 bonus advances, subject to the bonus max of ten.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There’s a ball save gate on the right outlane, activated by getting the right side lane in the mid-upper playfield. You can’t shoot it directly, but you can try to nudge the ball into it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The two stand-up add bonus targets above the return lanes are not worth shooting at. You should also avoid hitting the bumpers directly from the flippers, other than a grazing hit on the left edge of the left bumper.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Double bonus is scored on the last ball but cannot be lit on other balls.</p></div></section></div>
