<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Air Aces EM - Bally | 1975</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/d7a86062-665c-4c48-abfe-a72f8e0b5a1a-medium.jpg" alt="Air Aces backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">all drop targets have risk - - shots with the upper flippers risk side-to-side drains, shots with the lower flippers risk center drains and occasional side drains. It all depends on what angle you hit the target at and how hard. Balls exiting the top can go down the middle if they bounce off the rubber above the upper flippers and go past those flippers.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is the most important thing, maxes at 20K, can be doubled. The lit bumper advances it 1K as does each drop target. The bumper lights are changed by scoring ten-point switches. A key impact of this is that hitting the unlit bumper (worth 10 points) will light it, and hitting a lit bumper (worth 100\) will leave it lit.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Double bonus (and extra ball, if enabled) are collected in the upper saucers when lit. The bonus value to light them is operator-adjustable and can be anywhere from 4K to 13K.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Drops score 500; completing all 9 drops scores 5000. Drop targets do NOT reset when finished, they stay down until the next ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Exercise caution when shooting the drop targets with the upper mini-flippers. Such shots lead to side-to-side ball motion and risk draining out the sides. If you can’t help it, or if the rebounds prove to be safe, fine, otherwise just use them for deflecting balls up to the top. Learn to gauge when it’s safe to leave them down when the ball is falling down from above and let the ball roll to the lower flipper on the same side, or when to hold them up to deflect the ball so it lands on the opposite-side lower flipper. If it looks like the ball will do neither, then flip at it.</p></div></section></div>
