<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Big Deal EM - Williams | 1977</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/102a6fae-fc36-44f6-9ce2-df784bfbbe69-medium.jpg" alt="Big Deal backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD [left side only].</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Only the orbit is safe; drop target rebounds go wherever. Watch out for balls rolling slowly down the triangular feature above the B-I-G targets as well as balls dribbling slowly down the outside barrier of the left orbit and the right side of the horseshoe.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">“Big Deal” is a misnomer as far as the game’s name relates to tournament play. The “B-I-G-D-E-A-L” letters are for earning extra balls and replays, so they’re largely irrelevant in competition. The “big deal” for scoring on this game is the Aces. There are four Ace lanes at the top, and four more below, the two return lanes and the two outlanes. Each Ace lane adds 10K to your bonus. Getting all four Aces doubles your bonus. You also advance your bonus with each of the three red stand-up targets [left side of lower playfield; below and left of top Ace of Spades lane; and center upper right of horseshoe], as well as with the horseshoe shot. The horseshoe is also worth 10K for each Ace you’ve collected so far on that ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Given that all of this stuff is driven by getting the Aces, UTAD it is - - keep going there to max out your bonus and get all four Aces to double it. Once you’ve done that … just keep shooting the left orbit to the top for 5K per shot from the right flipper and heading towards the horseshoe from the left flipper. You can totally ignore the Big Deal letters for the entire game unless extra balls are on; if EBs are on, you still shouldn’t bother hitting the letters until your bonus is maxed out at 200K. Get the ball on the right flipper any way you can - dead bounce when possible; shatz left to right is pretty easy (this is a good game to learn to shatz on due to the width of the return lane exit).</p></div></section></div>
