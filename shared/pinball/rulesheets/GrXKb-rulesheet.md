<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Big Indian EM - Gottlieb | 1974</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/4498210c-7071-45be-ad5d-cf324f317a39-medium.jpg" alt="Big Indian backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD – top left drop targets from the right flipper, three star rollovers to the top from the left flipper.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Almost anything you do risks a center drain. Outlane drains come mainly from the slingshots or missed shots. Be especially wary of balls rolling down the outer right face of the vary-target chute and balls coming from the top right over the three star rollovers.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">A classic example of UTAD. The round stand-up targets are not worth the risk - - only 500 and one bonus advance. The vari-target is valuable enough to shoot [5K \+ 5 bonus advances if you hit it all the way back], but only worth risking if you’re really accurate; if you miss, the rebound is likely to drain. UTAD also gives you the chance to complete all three top lanes for double bonus.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you’ve maxed and doubled your bonus, then start shooting the vari-target.</p></div></section></div>
