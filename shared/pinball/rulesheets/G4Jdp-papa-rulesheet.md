CHECKPOINT
<br>
by Data East
<br>
Rulesheet by John Gantert
(<i><a href="mailto:jpg@mentor-tech.com">jpg@mentor-tech.com</a></i>)
</SPAN>
<p>
This rulesheet was based on A1.07 game ROMs and CP80 display ROMs, and is
not guaranteed to be correct, and it is definitely not complete! <p>
<span class="bodyTitle">LOWER PLAYFIELD:</span>
<P>
<i>Slingshots:</i>  Traditional style.  Scores 1,030 points.
Each hit to a slingshot alternates the outlane special and the
Spin-N-Win value.<p>
<i>Flippers:</i>  "Ultimate" flippers.  These are the Data East
solid state flippers.  Basically they are timed for high power,
then low power holds the flipper.  There is no EOS switch.
These flippers tend to be too
strong (even when in need of repair!) and break the drop targets!<p>
<i>Right inlane:</i>  Scores 20,000 points and lights spinner for
5,000 points per spin.<p>
<i>Left inlane:</i>  Scores 20,000 points and lights Nitro value.<p>
<i>Outlanes:</i>  Scores "BIG POINTS" and 2,000 miles.  Big Points
is an amount from about 25,000 to 100,000 points (I think it's
25,000 per ball played).  Also scores Special
when lit (Special alternates between outlanes based on flipper and
slingshot hits).<p>
<i>Pit Stop:</i>  Saucer just above the left inlane.  Scores 15,000
points.  When lit, this
will release a ball and start multi-ball.  When not lit to release a
ball it will randomly give an award (see below). <p>
<i>CARRERA:</i>  Seven drop targets located above the Pit Stop and below
the pop bumpers.  Each target is worth 25,000 points.  Completing all
seven drop targets scores 50,000 points, increases the RPM value,
and advances the jackpot value on the backbox.
It will also score the Blue Light Special if lit (see below).<p>
<i>Spin-N-Win:</i>  This is the spinner in between the two banks of
drop targets.  The spinner scores 1,010 points per spin and the lit
value which is changed by hits to the slingshots and pop bumpers.<p>
<i>Top Hole:</i>  Hole above the Flat Tire target that feeds the
upkicker.  Lights a L-A-P lane.<p>
<i>L-A-P Lanes:</i>  3 top inlanes.  Advances RPM and F-A-S-T when
flashing.  Lighting L-A-P also advances RPM and F-A-S-T.<p>
<i>F-A-S-T Lights:</i>  4 lights indicating progression towards FAST mode
(see below).<p>
<i>Pop Bumpers:</i>  3 located below the L-A-P Lanes.  Each hit scores
500 points and advances the Nitro Value by 500 points (see nitro
target).  40 (operator adjustable) pop bumper hits advances RPM.<p>
<i>Flat Tire:</i>  Switch protected by the pop bumpers.  Scores 25,000
points and increases pop bumper hits to 1,030 points (which also
increases the
advance of Nitro Value to 1,030 points!).  The pop bumpers
will flash after the Flat Tire target is hit to let you know they now
score more points.<p>
<i>Checkered Flag:</i>  Drop target located just in front of the
speed ramp.  It is raised during multi-ball and must be hit
before a jackpot can be scored.<p>
<i>Speed Ramp:</i>  This is the only ramp in the game.  It is located
just to the left of the Checkpoint.  It scores 100 points per MPH
(ie: 80 MPH = 8,000 points), and lights a country flag.
When all country flags are lit,
the ramp is lit for 1 Million points.  When all country flags are lit
consecutively (8 ramp shots in a row), the ramp is lit for 10 Million
points.  The speed of the ball up the ramp determines the MPH value.
When a certain MPH value
is exceeded (see display), the RPM is increased.  This ramp also
scores skill shot, extra ball and jackpot when lit.<p>
<i>Checkpoint:</i>  This is the rightmost lane that feeds to the
upkicker.  It releases a ball when lit and lights double
jackpot during multi-ball.  Scores 0 points when not lit.<p>
<i>Nitro Target:</i>  Target under the red Porsche located above the right
slingshot.  Scores 200,000 points when Hot Nitro is lit, collects pop
bumper value when blue light below car is lit, otherwise scores
5,000 points.<p>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">RULES</span>
<P>
<dl>
<dt><b>CHOOSE MUSIC:</b>
<dd>At the beginning of the first ball, the player can select
which style of music should be played during the game.
Use the flippers to select the type within 13 seconds,
then plunge the ball to start.  Choices include:<p>
<ul>
<li>Country
<li>Rock
<li>Jazz
<li>Rap
<li>Classical
<li>Soul
</ul><p>
<dt><b>SKILL SHOT:</b>
<dd>Plunge ball.  When the upkicker returns the ball to the left
flipper, shoot the ramp.  You have 5 seconds to do this, and you must
not hit any other target before hitting the ramp.  This scores
250,000 times the ball in play.  (ie:  250,000 on ball 1; 500,000
on ball 2; and 750,000 on ball 3)<p>
<dt><b>BALL SAVER:</b>
<dd>Hit only 1 or 2 switches, and you will get another ball (and
another skill shot!).<p>
<dt><b>BONUS:</b>
<dd>Bonus is the number of miles.  Outlanes score 2,000 miles. <p>
<dt><b>BONUS MULTIPLIER:</b>
<dd>Advancing RPMs advances the Bonus Multiplier up to 8X.<p>
<dt><b>PIT STOP AWARDS:</b>
<dd>Random awards include:<p>
<ul>
<li>Light Outlane Special
<li>Light Extra Ball
<li>Mystery Point Value
<li>Advance FAST
<li>Miles Hold (ie: Bonus Hold)
<li>Increase RPM<p>
</ul>
<dt><b>GENERAL RULES:</b>
<dd>Increase RPM to start multi-ball.<p>
<dl>
<dt><b>TO INCREASE RPM:</b>
<dd>RPM can be increased by 1000 several ways.  These include:
<ul>
<li>Pit Stop random awards.
<li>Shooting the spinner when Increase RPM is lit.
<li>Completing the LAP lanes.
<li>Completing CARRERA drop targets.
<li>40 (operator adjustable) pop bumper hits.
<li>Quick shot to the ramp going more than XXX (see display)
MPH.
</ul>
</dl>
</dl>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">MULTIBALL</span>
<P>
Increase RPM to 6000 and shoot the Checkpoint, or increase RPM to
7000 and shoot either the Checkpoint or the Pit Stop to start 2 ball
multi-ball.  Increase the RPM to 8000 to start 3 ball multi-ball
(RedLine).<p>
<dl>
<dt><b>2 BALL MULTIBALL:</b>
<dd>Shoot Checkpoint or Pit Stop (when lit) to release another
ball and start 3 Ball Multiball (below).<p>
<dt><b>3 BALL MULTIBALL (RED LINE):</b>
<dd>Shoot Checkered Flag single drop target to light jackpot.
Jackpot is lit for about 5 seconds.  Shoot ramp during this time
to score the Jackpot.<p>
<dd>Jackpot value is determined by the flashing light on the backbox.
Jackpot values are 2 Million, 3 Million, and 4 Million.  The
value is advanced by completing the CARRERA drop targets.  Note
that if the Jackpot is at 4 Million and you complete the drop
targets, the jackpot will move down to 2 Million!  Also,
shooting Checkpoint when the Jackpot is not lit and when
all three balls are still in play will
double the jackpot value the next time the jackpot is lit.<p>
<dd>After one ball is lost (2 balls in play), shooting the Checkpoint
will light Checkpoint (and Pit Stop?) to release another ball.<p>
</dl>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">MODES</span>
<P>
All modes are timed.  There is a small timer located on the DMD,
and when it hits 0:00, it's over.  There's no grace period at all.
You know when a mode is going to end, by hearing the ticking sound.  When
the ticking sound stops, the mode is over!<p>
<dl>
<dt><b>FAST:</b>
<dd>To start fast mode, first complete the LAP lanes which advances
the FAST lights.  After FAST is lit, shoot the Checkpoint to
start the mode!<p>
In this mode, every switch is worth 55,000 points, but mode
only lasts for 10 seconds!  So to make it worth your
while, you need to shoot the spinner or the pop bumpers.
It also helps to have multi-ball going when this mode starts.<p>
All FAST scoring gets added to your score at the end
of the ball in play, after the bonus is added.<p>
<dt><b>BLUE LIGHT SPECIAL:</b>
<dd>This mode randomly appears after you hit down a drop target.
You get 10 seconds to hit down the remaining drop targets for
a random award plus 500,000 points.<p>
The random awards include:
<ul>
<li>1 Million Points
<li>Red Line
<li>Extra Ball
<li>Take High Score
<li>Double Score
</ul><p>
<dt><b>1 MILLION RAMP / 10 MILLION RAMP:</b>
<dd>The ramp is lit for 10 seconds.  Shooting the ramp scores
1 or 10 million points (depending on mode).  On some machines
(operator adjustable) you may be able to repeatedly shoot
the ramp for 1 million each shot for 10 seconds.  The 10 Million
shot can not be repeatedly shot for 10 Million points.<p>
<dt><b>HOT NITRO ROUND</b>
<dd>This round is active while the yellow Hot Nitro light
is lit.  During this round, the Nitro Target scores 200,000
points, and 20 MPH are added to all ramp shots.<p>
