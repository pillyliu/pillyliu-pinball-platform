<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Old Coney Island! SS - Game Plan | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/420ea981-3039-430b-a466-24e8a6252f01-medium.jpg" alt="Old Coney Island! backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the left saucer to light the spinner and advance your bonus multiplier, then UTAD through the spinner.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls coming down from behind the center targets on the left can drain or hit the left slingshot. Balls coming down the “E” lane can center drain depending on exit speed. Drop target shots have rebound risk. Spinner or star rollover shots that don’t go all the way up can fall back to center drain. Balls never drain out the right side unless by a missed shot or left slingshot action.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Somewhat a mirror image of their Sharpshooter game, it has the same main seven drop target bank where each target adds to your bonus, a multiplier saucer, at the middle left in this case.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus maxes at 29K base and 5X for 145K total.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The highest value shot on the game is their standard 50K hard-to-enter lane, here on the mid-upper right side where the ball’s most likely way into the lane is via a lucky bounce off the lower bumper. Yes, try to nudge it in when it’s in that area, it’s big points on this game.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The C-O-N-E-Y letters also advance bonus, but only the first time each is scored, so don’t worry about them.</p></div></section></div>
