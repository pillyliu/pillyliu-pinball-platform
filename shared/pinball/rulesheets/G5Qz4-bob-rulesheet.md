<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Rolling Stones SS - Bally | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/5c7689c7-3d0c-4232-8d05-bc49f18495db-medium.jpg" alt="Rolling Stones backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> RS</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Saucer at the top left almost exclusively. Nudge balls in the bumper area to collect targets \#1-4, then shoot the center \#5 target to advance your super bonus. Repeat, but you’ll need to hit the post inside either horseshoe to relight the \#5 target.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumper exits lead in loss of control. Once in a while the drop target at the upper left will rebound down the middle or into a slingshot. Rebounds from the center \#5 target are risky. The arc shots at the lower sides are safe unless the ball grazes the slingshot after coming out of them.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Why do I say top saucer all day? The saucer collects bonus and ejects the ball to the top of the machine to collect a ROCK letter. You need to knock down the drop target protecting the saucer first. R-O-C-K lane completions advance bonus multiplier to 2X, 3X, 5X. Bonus X is preserved to subsequent balls.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing 1-2-3-4-5 advances the super bonus to 20K, 40K and 60K. The \#5 target also scores a ROCK letter when lit. Hitting one of the posts inside either side horseshoe shot relights the \#5 target. Regular bonus maxes out at 19K and resets each ball; the 20-40-60K bonus is separate and is held to future balls. With 5X and max bonus, that’s 395K for a collect bonus shot.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The drop target bank on the upper right scores a variable value when completed: the point lights behind them indicate the value to be scored; you want to get the last target to finish the bank when it’s worth 25K if you can. The value cycles quickly from 5000 at the bottom upward. Partially completed banks are preserved to the next ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Horseshoe shots in either direction score the current value and raise the value for the next shot up to a maximum of 10K plus 5 bonus advances.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If the ball is on the left flipper and you’re not comfortable trying to transfer it to the right, go up top towards the \#3 and \#4 targets to try to get those and have the ball bang around in the bumpers and hopefully also get \#1 and \#2. Of course, you can shoot \#5 when lit with the left flipper, too.</p></div></section></div>
