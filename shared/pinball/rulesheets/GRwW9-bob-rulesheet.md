<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Dealer&#x27;s Choice EM - Williams | 1974</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/720a5b5c-b4c5-46c7-a86e-472504014c6d-medium.jpg" alt="Dealer&#x27;s Choice backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the A-K-Q-J-10 stand-ups from both the top and bottom right flippers; shoot the 1000/Special lane next to the upper flipper from the left flippers. You’ll get the 1 through 9 center rollover disks shooting crosswise at the cards from the upper flipper, although if you just need one or two to finish the set, go ahead and use a bottom flipper.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">All the shot rebounds are risky, and you’ll be doing a lot of side-to-side shooting plus getting lots of rebounds off the bottom of the bumpers. Stay alert\!</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This is the replay version of Lucky Ace, with the same high degree of side-to-side action.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is a major factor here. Base bonus maxes at 10000 and is advanced by either top lane and by the three yellow stand-up targets. Specials are worth 5000 and are earned two ways: completing 1 through 9 three or more times or completing 10-J-Q-K-A and shooting the special lane above the upper right flipper. Completing the five cards also lights the return lane on the right for an extra ball, if enabled.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">As for the 1-9 targets, crosswise shots using the upper flipper are a good way to collect those, and you will usually get a card at the same time. The rebound off of the card stand-ups usually falls down towards one of the flippers rather than making it all the way across to the right outlane, although it can arc down the center at times.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">From the lower flippers, shoot up the Special lane or into the cards. Shooting the 1-9 disks is a bit risky since after the ball goes over them it will probably hit and trigger one of the bumpers, which can lead to a drain down the center or either side.</p></div></section></div>
