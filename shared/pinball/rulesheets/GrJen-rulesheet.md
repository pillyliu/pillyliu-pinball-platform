<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Dodge City EM - Gottlieb | 1965</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/a1899373-f681-487e-aec4-add53433c7e9-medium.jpg" alt="Dodge City backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the “rollunder” gates in order and the lit saucer.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">The least risky shots are the two saucers and the A and E gates, though missing the A and E shots is easy to do due to the angle involved. The B, C and D gates have some risk from the bumpers behind them that can smack the ball back at you. With all the posts and the fact that 2” flippers are harder to aim, expect a lot of missed shots hitting those posts.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The points here are mostly in the rollunder gates A-E and the two side saucers. You need to shoot the rollunders in order; only the one you need next will be lit. Completing all 5 raises their value by a factor of 10. Doing so again raises the value by a factor of 100. The base value of each gate is set by a spinning Roto wheel set underneath the playfield; all you can see of it are five numbers in small windows in the playfield, one below each rollunder, ranging in value from 2 to 5, plus a star worth 1 that awards an extra ball. Once you have the value at 100X, the next rollunder you hit will score 100 times the value in the window under it, then reset the base value to 1.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">One of the two side saucers will be lit at any time. At 50, 100 or 150 points per shot, that’s good value when lit; ignore whichever one is not lit. Changing the tens digit of the score swaps which saucer is lit.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Each rollunder shot lights the bumper above it for 10 instead of 1 point. The center bumper is always worth 10.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If the ball starts to drain out either side, nudge to get it into whichever outlane is worth 50 and spin roto.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Make either of the two center lanes that score 50 and spin the Roto.</p></section></div>
