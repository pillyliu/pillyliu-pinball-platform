<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Count-Down SS - Gottlieb | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/53d09b02-c41c-45f6-889d-98111c48045c-medium.jpg" alt="Count-Down backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Drop target banks all day, but in color order green, yellow, red, blue the first time through. If you get the top saucer lit for collect bonus with 4X or 5X, take it if you can.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">It’s almost all rebound risk from drop target shots. Shooting the green and yellow targets with the bottom flippers is the riskiest.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you like drop targets, this is your game. The scoring is all drops and bonus built up from them. Drop target value for a given color bank goes up from 500 each to 5000 each once you’ve completed the set once on a ball, so blast away\! As for the bonus multiplier, this is one of the “specific order” games: finishing the green targets gives you 2X, green+yellow gives 3X, green+yellow+red gives 4X, all four colors gives 5X. But if you have yellow, red and blue without green complete, your bonus is still 1X. Your order of attack is clearly indicated here.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">When hitting the drops, watch out for center drain rebounds off of the green and yellow targets, especially when shot from the bottom flippers. Using the top flippers gives you more of a sideways angle on them for a usually safer rebound.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Once your base bonus is up to 20K, the top saucer lights for collect bonus. As usual for these Gottlieb games, you don’t want to collect unless your bonus multiplier is high, since the bonus value resets upon collection, although the multiplier stays for the rest of that ball. If your bonus is at 4X or 5X, try to collect it if the ball goes up top near the saucer. You can only get to the top saucer using the upper mini flippers, bank shots, or nudges off of the top of the bumper; the bumper blocks shots from the lower flippers.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If extra balls are enabled, completing 1 or 2 target banks [operator settable choice] will light the EB at the upper left lane. My shot for getting the extra ball is to use the lower left flipper and bank the ball off of the second blue target from the top: this often ricochets nicely into the EB lane\!</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Center Post note: Countdowns come with a rubbered center post, but the insert for “same player shoots again” that’s just above the post is often so sunken that a ball hitting the supposed-to-rebound-into-play post won’t do so, instead hanging up on the insert’s edge, then falling back to drain. It may also bounce just enough to get stuck on the insert’s lower edge just above the post. Sadly, balls there are considered drained. Also, the post might be removed for competition. Either way, don’t rely on the post to save your ball; if it does, count yourself fortunate.</p></div></section></div>
