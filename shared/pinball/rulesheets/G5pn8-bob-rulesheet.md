<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Pokerino SS - Williams | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/11f14861-3712-40c1-a381-fcc83c4fd725-medium.jpg" alt="Pokerino backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Get the upper left horseshoe for bonus multiplier right away. After that, shoot drop targets.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Bias Right initially, then mild bias left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">All target shots and bumper action are risky. Most Pokerinos play a bit “floaty” (slow), giving you more time to anticipate ball movements.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Each card type is earned a different way: Aces by the top four star rollovers, Kings by hitting the upper left captive ball, Queens by the upper right drop target 4-bank, Jacks by the two lower side drop target pairs, and tens by the single star rollover above the center triangle that alternates suits. Completing any card rank earns 10K and advances you Pokerino lights (except for Kings). Jacks and Queens target banks are worth 20K the second time and thereafter, individual King hits 10K after completed. Card completions are held from ball to ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is earned for each card collected and target hit up to 29K. Bonus multiplier is earned by making the top left horseshoe shot up to 5X for 145K total. Base bonus resets each ball but the multiplier is held.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The double flippers take some getting used to, and scissors drains are a constant danger. The game is so wide that shots at the Jack targets are fairly safe; the ball almost never rebounds to the other side drain. Avoid the center drop target in that triangle by the tens.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Due to the held multiplier, the best strategy is to get to 5X right away, then go for the J and Q drop targets to build bonus and raise the bank values to 20K. Shoot the left spinner if you light it.</p></div></section></div>
