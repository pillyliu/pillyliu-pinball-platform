<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Tag-Team Pinball SS - Gottlieb | 1985</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/fd2b4d2e-cbad-4e94-b776-5fe9ed783aab-medium.jpg" alt="Tag-Team Pinball backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Complete TAG and TEAM targets to lock balls, play multiball. During multiball, UTAD, through the lit spinner when possible.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">T-A-G rebounds can center drain if hit wrong. T-E-A-M rebounds can go to the left slingshot. Balls exiting the top to the sides around the center drop target bank can go to the slingshots. If a spinner shot fails to go to the top, watch out if it hits the tiny post rubber below the spinner as it falls back. All saucer kickouts are usually safe.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This game is often underrated; it’s a lot of fun to play with an unusual playfield layout and a nice set of rules.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Complete the T-A-G drop targets in left to right order to light the upper left lock. Complete the lower right T-E-A-M drop targets in order to light the lower right lock. Watch out for the rebounds off the TEAM targets.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Once both balls are locked, shoot the “Release” horseshoe on the left to start multiball. Multiball scores 3X playfield. Now, go up top. The left return lane lights the spinner for 10K; that makes a lit spinner 30K during multiball, half a million on a good rip. With the right flipper, shoot for the top left lock saucer and then when the ball kicks out of that, shoot for the gate to the top lanes to advance your bonus multiplier and work towards a special.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">One nice feature of Tag Team is that a locked ball serves as a potential ball save\! If you drain out the lit left “last chance” outlane with one ball locked, the locked ball is released and play continues.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus goes up to 29K, not 39K. Every Tag Team I’ve played or watched online has a bug in the bonus value lights. When you go from 19K to 20K in bonus, the 10K light stays on. No, you haven’t got 30K. While the 1K-9K lights will still go on to indicate progress from 20K to 29K and are additive to the 20K, the 10K is not additive.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus multiplier goes up to 5X, increased by completing the top three lanes. Once base bonus reaches 20K, one of the lock saucers or the release will light to collect bonus (with multiplier). Only one is lit at any time, cycling between these three shots on slingshot hits. After a bonus collect, the base resets but the multiplier remains.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This game has the lane light indicators reversed from normal: you need to have the ball go through the lit lane to unlight it\!</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Extra balls and specials should be off and award 500K instead; that makes them very worth getting\! You can get the special in the lit top lane from any of the three flippers: from the lower left via either the spinner, the upper left by shooting through the gate right of the “G” target, and the lower right by shooting the gap left of the “T” in TAG. There’s also a special stand-up target above the upper flipper. For the Extra Ball, it’s that shot left of the “T”, lit by making multiple timed shots through it immediately after the ball goes through the right return lane (there is no right outlane).</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">While the TAG targets are best shot with the upper flipper, you’ll find that it’s easy to get them from the bottom right flipper via bank shots off the bullseye stand-up target. You can sometimes backhand TAG letters from the lower left flipper. You can backhand the top lock from the upper flipper.</p></div></section></div>
