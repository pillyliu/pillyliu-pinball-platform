<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Strato-Flite EM - Williams | 1974</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/1b52964f-c3c9-42ee-9e26-5ae2155c65eb-medium.jpg" alt="Strato-Flite backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD, via bank shots off the A and D stand-up banks.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">All plunges and most flipper shots will end up in the bumpers, from which the ball goes wherever. The few shots that rebound straight enough to avoid the bumpers will usually hit the slingshots instead anyway. The one exception is the center stand-up target, which can rebound drain immediately. Be on guard at all times.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing a set of letters [A, B, C or D] increases your bonus multiplier, one set for 2X, two sets for 3X. Additional sets only lead to special, not worth anything in tournament play; focus on completing two sets.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If the lower gate is open, don’t try to avoid that gate if the ball is heading that way, take the gate and 5000 points plus the chance to plunge back to the top.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Balls coming down the left return lane almost always do so fast enough to pass them over to the right flipper for a more controlled shot up top.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">land in the top saucer, ideally for open bottom gate [1st choice] or 5000 points [2nd choice]; open upper gate is okay, but the ball goes through that far less often than the bottom gate; 500 points is the worst option. The small slings on either side of the saucer at the top cycle its value, so try to get the optimal number of back-and-forth bounces on them to light the gate choice. The right-hand sling also scores a bonus advance, so multiple back-and-forths leading to a drop into the saucer are better than a clean drop into it.</p></section></div>
