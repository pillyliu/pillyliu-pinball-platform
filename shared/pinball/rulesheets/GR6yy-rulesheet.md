<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">El Toro EM - Bally | 1972</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/bfb32cb6-f9c5-472f-99aa-f1353a53cc54-medium.jpg" alt="El Toro backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">shoot the mushroom bumpers to light the added values in the chute at the right, then UTAD.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumper exits and smackdowns; the left lane and right chute feeds are usually safe. The mushroom bumper shots are risky but necessary; try to graze their edges rather than hit them face-on.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Right flipper: try to graze the “light \#1” mushroom bumper on the right side to light that lane on the right side of the machine; after that, it’s UTAD and nudge for the 1000 lanes.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Left flipper: try to graze “light \#2” mushroom bumper on the right side and \#3 on the left side to light those lanes. Once two or more lanes are lit, shoot to the upper right for the chute lane. [One of your misses might light the third lane anyway.]</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the El Toro letters otherwise unless extra balls are on and playable. In that case, try to finish the El Toro letters to light the top center lane to earn an extra ball. The extra ball lane will not always be lit even after you finish the letters; it switches on and off with the tens digit of your score.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Grazing the mushroom bumpers is better than hitting them face on; face shots can rebound in dangerous ways, while grazing hits will continue up the playfield.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If the right flipper is able to backhand the right side chute and the feed out of it is good, try repeating that shot as much as possible.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">center lane, then one of the lit 1000 lanes.</p></section></div>
