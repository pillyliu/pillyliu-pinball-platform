<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Dolly Parton SS - Bally | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/62fe2cf4-0ff1-4323-af44-5c9101605a49-medium.jpg" alt="Dolly Parton backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> DP</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Lit spinner all day if juicy; the first inline drop target lights it. If not juicy, continue inline drop targets until bonus at 5X from the left flipper, then UTAD through the spinner. From the right flipper, transfer to the left if able, especially with a juicy spinner, otherwise UTAD to the left of the bumpers.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Most drains come from the bumpers or when the ball rolls down the left side below the D-O-L-L-Y letters. Rebounds from the inline targets can sometimes center drain, but you need at least the first one; hitting a post on either side of the inline targets is worse, don’t miss\!</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The first inline drop target lights your spinner - do that immediately\! If the spinner is juicy, i.e. it registers many spins per shot (ideally 20+, but at least 10-15), one viable strategy is just shoot the lit spinner all day. The other high point value option is to finish the inline drop targets to get your bonus multiplier up to 5X; the second target gets you 2X, the third 3X, the last one 5X. The stand-up target behind the inline drops scores 20K, but it’s a tight shot and misses can drain, so at that point, just use the left flipper for the spinner even if not juicy. On many Dollys you can backhand the drop targets from the right flipper; try it\!</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">End of ball base bonus is 2K per letter in “Dolly Parton” collected. You get Parton letters from the top saucer and the inline drop targets, Dolly letters from the stand-up targets at the upper left. If you finish them all, you get a “super bonus” of 22K locked in for the rest of the game and can start a second set of letters. There’s another lock-in at two sets for 44K. After that, completing the letters scores a special and resets them; don’t do that unless you get points for specials. Your maximum bonus is then [ [ 44K \+ 10x2K ] times 5X ] or 320,000. Since the 22K and 44K values hold from ball to ball, you’ll need to get these early in the game to get a top score in qualifying unless that spinner is really juiced. My general technique for collecting letters is just fling the ball at the slingshot left of the bumpers and try to nudge the bounces into the Dolly letters, the top saucer, or both, before the ball comes back down. Since the bumpers block direct shots at most of the Dolly letters, nudging skill can be more important than shot accuracy on this game.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the 5000 lane just below the spinner; if a ball goes through there, fine, but it’s not worth risking shots at.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Format note: in “best game” format, it’s important to get your DOLLY PARTON letters and 5X multiplier for the potential 320K end of ball bonus. In match play, assuming the spinner is reasonably active, shooting the spinner once lit is usually the better way to go rather than shooting the additional in-line drop targets for bonus multipliers.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">make the top saucer. That gives you a letter in P-A-R-T-O-N, advancing your bonus. It’s one of the harder Skill Shots due to the wide open spaces on both sides of the saucer; more than half of people’s plunges fail to drop into the saucer.</p></section></div>
