<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Gigi EM - Gottlieb | 1963</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/5a884657-22bc-4099-b743-c5099c25abe9-medium.jpg" alt="Gigi backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD; try to complete each color set of bumpers.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Do your best to thread your shots between the bumpers so the ball hits them on the way down rather than up. Feeds from the bumpers and side lanes can drain or go out of control into the slingshots. Bumper smack-down drains are common. Lots of subtle nudging required.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Rarely used due to its high luck level, Gigi is all about completing either set of bumpers on a single ball. This gives you an end-of-game (not end-of-ball\!) bonus; 100 for one set, 200 for 2 sets, 300 for 3 or more sets. Bumpers reset each ball; 6 out of 7 of each color on all five balls gets you zero bonus.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy is super simple: thread the needle between the 2-3, 3-4, 4-5 or 5-6 red bumpers as often as you can and nudge for maximum bumper action before the ball falls back down. Ignore the 5 green rollovers. When the ball is up top, try to get some hits on the stand-up targets above the yellow \#2 and \#6 bumpers.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Draining “correctly” is important; the outlanes score 100, the center nada. You can win or lose a game with side drains.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: the rolls out of the two side lanes.</p></div></section></div>
