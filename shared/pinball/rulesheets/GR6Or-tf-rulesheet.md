As people figure out the rules, they can add/change/remove them as needed until the rulesheet becomes pretty complete. Over time this would be a pretty cool archive of games. <h2>RULES GO HERE</h2>
<p>Apparently bonus is valuable.</p>
<p>Shoot either unlit ramp to enter the ring. Not sure of mode scoring, apparently it can be lit by completing the WWE spell out.</p>
<p>Complete the 5 rollover lanes to advance the Bonus Multiplier.</p>
<p>Shoot lit purple REF shots to begin Referee Multiball or Referee Scoring (modes alternate like Vengeance modes in ST). During the former, shoot lit shots to score 250k Jackpots. During the latter, shoot the left ramp to collect a Hurry-Up value starting at 1m. Shoot it 5 times to complete the mode (?)</p>
<p>Shoot 10 loops to Light Extra Ball and advance towards Referee modes.</p>
<p>Complete Tag-Team standups, then shoot the left scoop followed by the ramp to begin Tag Team Multiball. Not sure of Jackpot scoring yet.</p>
<p>After pinning opponent, moving showboat targets awards 200K+, after hitting the saucer during showboat, the showboat target seem to award 2M+ each??</p>
<p>Once you complete a match by pinning an opponent you need to hit all three WWE drops to relight match. Shooting the ball up either ramp starts the new match.</p>
