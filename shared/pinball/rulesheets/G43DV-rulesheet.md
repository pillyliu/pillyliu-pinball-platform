<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Ice Fever SS - Gottlieb | 1985</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/ef792bb4-54f9-4a13-b619-ecf0cbe706d7-medium.jpg" alt="Ice Fever backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via the lit spinner on the right.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">The bumpers, of course; you’ll be shooting the orbits that feed the top lanes and then the bumpers; what happens when the ball is done in the bumpers will determine how long each ball lasts.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">*Shot to master: Spinner.*</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The lit return lane (alternates) lights the spinner for 10K per spin instead of 1K. Shooting it dominates all other strategies on the game.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus goes up to 29K, scored by any lane. Bonus multiplier goes up to 5X for 145K maximum total. Three goals (a hat trick) increases the bonus multiplier one level. Shoot the drop targets in front of the captive ball to expose it, hit the captive ball to score a goal.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing the top three I-C-E lanes lights one of them for an extra ball which scores 100K; the lit EB lane cycles with switch hits.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Basic strategy is to do whatever it takes to light the spinner, then do whatever it takes to set up shots to it on the left flipper. Shatzing either way is a good way to do this.</p></div></section></div>
