<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Skylab EM - Williams | 1974</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/90c24fd9-71e0-4d6d-8346-e7f93b63e49c-medium.jpg" alt="Skylab backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD, via banking the ball off any SKYLAB letters you need.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">The ball will be going up and down the sides all the time; your goal is to manage the angle of return to avoid the slingshots, center drain and outlanes. Easier said than done. If the ball looks headed for an outlane, try to get it to hit the angled rubber above the return lane to get it to go there instead.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There’s not much here to shoot - - the captive ball and UTAD via the S-K-Y-L-A-B letters are about it, and the latter is the less drainy of the two choices. Each time you complete Skylab you get two bonus advances; each lit lane at the top and the return lanes give you one advance. The little red rollover buttons labeled “change” alternate which lanes are lit for an advance. Bonus maxes at 24K and is doubled on the last ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the captive balls unless the Special award is active and good for either an extra ball or significant points - - check the instruction card or watch other players to see. If it’s on, getting all 5 balls to the lit side scores it. If not, even after maxing out your bonus, UTAD is still safer than shooting the captive ball.</p></div></section></div>
