<span class="bodyTitle">INTRODUCTION:</span>
<P>
This document is being worked on as I am working on the machine. As of

<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">LAYOUT:</span>
<P>
This game has a somewhat unique playfield that borders on 3 levels.
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">MAIN LEVEL:</SPAN>
<P>
<ul><li>2 flippers in standard location
<li>Bank of 3 targets on the left
<li>Bank of 5 targets on the right
<li>Bank of 3 drop targets upper right
<li>2 jet bumpers upper middle
<li>Ball save / Instant multi-ball left outlane
<li>Lifter to left side of second level
<li>Lifter to right side of second level
<li>Ramp to second level
</ul>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">SECOND LEVEL:</SPAN>
<P>
The second level is in 4 parts, the left side, and the upper and lower right sides
and a raised area. There is a bridge from the left side angling up to the upper
right side. The bridge and about half of the second level have no barriers to keep
the ball from falling to the main level.
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">SECOND LEVEL - LEFT:</SPAN>
<P>
<ul><li>Drop hole - to left side lifter right back to second level.
<li>Flipper - Used for a skill shot across the bridge.
</ul>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">SECOND LEVEL - RIGHT, UPPER:</SPAN>
<P>
<ul><li>Flipper - Used for skill shots located on RAISED area.
<li>Star rollover - skill shot from left flipper.
</ul>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">SECOND LEVEL - RIGHT, LOWER:</SPAN>
<P>
This area has 2 ways for the ball to travel, the first simply drops
the ball to the main play field while the second is a long slide that
drops the ball to the lower right flipper and increments the multi-ball
counter.
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">SECOND LEVEL - RAISED:</SPAN>
<P>
The raised area is a roughly 5" high ramp. Unlike most ramps, this one
has 2 skill shots that go behind the normal playfield. If the ball goes
past both of these shots, it is returned to the left upper flipper.
The first skill shot adds to the treasure value and returns to the upper
right flipper. The second skill shot is to collect treasure value and
must be opened on the main level before the shot is allowed.
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">RULE CARDS:</SPAN>
<P>
The rules as found on the game are broken up onto two stickers,
1 each on the left and right of the machine. These are located on a
formed area and as such I am not able to scan them. With this in mind
I have attempted to capture the feel of these cards below.

<b>ESCAPE FROM THE LOST WORLD</b>
<ul>
<li>BUILD TREASURE VALUE<br>
By shooting at Idols, Cave, Volcano & Skeleton.
Treasure value appears on backglass displays.
<li>OPEN ESCAPE GATE
<P>
By completing drop target bank.
<li> ESCAPE TO COLLECT TREASURE
<P>
Cross BRIDGE OF GOLD to set up ESCAPE flipper.
ESCAPE by shooting through flashing gateway at upper
left.
</ul>
M05-00365-A084
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">RIGHT RULE CARD:</SPAN>
<P>
<ul>
<li> MULTI-BALL RELEASE COUNTER
<P>
Advance the MULTI-BALL RELEASE COUNTER to 3 to release
AUTOMATIC MULTI-BALL. TOP SAUCER and RIGHT and SECOND
LEVEL FLIPPER RETURN advance the counter by 1.
<li>INSTANTANEOUS MULTI-BALL
<P>
BALL SAVER & ESCAPE GATEWAY AWARD instantaneous
multi-ball!
<li>HOLD BONUS
<P>
Make center cave shot, when flashing.
<li> EXTRA BALL
<P>
Volcano star roll-over lights Extra Ball
Skeleton collects Extra Ball, when flashing
<li> SPECIAL
<P>
Make right cave shot, when flashing.
</ul>
