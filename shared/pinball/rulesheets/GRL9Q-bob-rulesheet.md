<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Pop-A-Card EM - Gottlieb | 1972</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/7607c1f3-47d0-4911-a88f-6353668928b3-medium.jpg" alt="Pop-A-Card backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">complete the drop targets, then UTAD, trying to get the ball to go through a 5000 lane at the upper sides.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">All target rebounds are risky, but that’s all there is to do. Watch out for balls falling through the top right lane, hitting the right bumper, rebounding into the slingshot above and right of the bumper, then draining down the middle. Another common top lane drain is through the top left lane falling onto the round corner rubber above the \#5 target, then rebounding out the right outlane.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This is the add-a-ball version of Drop-A-Card with different scoring.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The Skill Shot is the lit top lane; one side will always be lit, so soft plunge for the right side, find the right plunger marker for the left. As the ball drops through the top lane towards either bumper, try to nudge it into the lane to the outside for 500 or 5000 points.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot sets of drops on the sides to light the bumpers and lanes on the same side for increased values.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy depends on whether or not add-a-balls are on. If they are, complete the two side target banks to light each side’s respective stand-up target for extra balls. Complete the top 5 targets to light the top side lanes and outlanes for extra balls. The extra ball targets stay on for the remainder of the ball in play, so you can run the balls-to-play counter up to a maximum of 10 if you keep hitting them.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If extra balls are off, you want to focus on getting the two top lanes for 5000 and not finish the 10 through Ace targets, since doing so changes the value of those lanes from 5K to zero. Just go up top and try to nudge the ball around into either of the lanes. When the ball is at the bottom, hit the two stand-up targets for 1K and avoid hitting the drop targets that would turn them into “wow”s worth zero.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Try to anticipate side drains to get the ball to rattle out of them. In particular, note the strength of kicks by the two slingshots and try to nudge if a sling kick looks like it will be just enough to arc into the opposite outlane.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Control vs. Flow Alert\! The flippers on this game are rarely strong enough to make shots from a cradle, so plan on playing flow style and using the ball’s momentum to your advantage.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-orange mb-1">Style Alert:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line italic">Go with the Flow\!</p></section></div>
