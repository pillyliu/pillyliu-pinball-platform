<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Superman SS - Atari | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/28ae6d91-56c4-4e0a-bcee-b38f2e3b5490-medium.jpg" alt="Superman backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via the spinners; option to shoot lit center drop targets. If extra balls are on, also go for the center drop targets when lit.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Mild bias Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">High; spinners are safest; watch where rebounds off the center drop targets go from both flippers, choose the safer one to shoot with for each; see Key Feeds, too</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The biggest points on the game is for spelling Superman – 30K or 50K depending on settings. The catch is, three of the letters are return lanes and the other five are somewhat risky-rebound stand-up targets. While it’s good value, it’s not worth the risk to go for it unless you only need one or two of the five stand-ups.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is earned by all lanes, star rollovers and drop targets; the top lanes give as many advances as the lane number; outlanes give two. Bonus goes up to 29K. To get double bonus, complete lanes 1, 2 and 3 up top; for triple bonus, also get lane 4. Lanes are preserved to the next ball unless you get to triple bonus, after which they reset.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">As soon as you hit any slingshot, one of the spinners will light for 1000 per spin; which spinner is lit toggles with subsequent slingshot hits. Shots through the left spinner give an extra 5K at the top.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The center drop targets, worth 1K plus a bonus advance, will always have one position lit for 5K; which one is lit cycles back and forth on a timer. Try to get each of the targets when lit if you shoot them. The rebounds from the center drop targets often head towards the right outlane, though, so be careful\!</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing the five drop targets raises the upper left saucer value from 5K to 10K; a second set advances it to extra ball if enabled. Getting the M-A-N letters lights the star rollover left of the left flipper for extra ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: balls coming down the right spinner and balls kicked out of the top left saucer.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">For strategy, the spinners are usually safer than the drop targets and the left one when lit with that extra 5K rollover at the top is your best risk-reward bet. If you find the rebounds from the center drops to be not too bad, you can shoot them from the left flipper. I’d stick with the left spinner, even if not lit, from the right flipper, though.</p></div></section></div>
