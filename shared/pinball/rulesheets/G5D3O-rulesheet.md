<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Lucky Seven SS - Williams | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/56ad40f8-9682-417f-8dac-6a9ef6c18dee-medium.jpg" alt="Lucky Seven backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Up top through the almost-useless spinner to advance bonus and change the reels. When reel value is high, shoot the drop targets to collect it.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Bias Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls rolling off of the central semi-oval mounting that hit the rubbers below the drop targets to center drain or go into the slingshot, or balls that just barely drip off the edge and hit the outside top of a slingshot mount to deflect into an outlane. The targets all have rebound risk, too.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is advanced 3000 at a time by the four top lanes, two return lanes and upper left stand-up target. Bonus goes up to 30K, so you only need 10 advances to max it out. Bonus can be doubled to 60K (see below).</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The game’s primary scoring, other than bonus, is based on the current “reel value” like on Williams’ Jackpot from 1971. The symbols on the three reels change when you score the corresponding color-aligned top lanes or center stand-up targets.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">You collect the reel value by completing the two drop targets on the sides of the game. To double your bonus, you need exactly one or two clovers among the three symbols. One clover scores 5K; 2 clovers earns 10K; 3 clovers 20K and an extra ball if enabled; three horseshoes is 20K; three diamonds or three sevens is 30K. Other combinations of symbols score nothing other than the 1000 for hitting each drop target.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Either of the return lanes will light the spinner, but even lit it’s only worth 100.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy is to shoot the drop targets whenever the reel value is 20K or 30K, otherwise go up top to build bonus. Once your bonus is high enough, shoot the reel targets when the double bonus light is on. If you need to change exactly one reel to get to a desired combination, shoot the necessary center stand-up target. Otherwise, avoid those targets since rebounds off them or misses risk draining. If you’ve maxed and doubled your bonus, it’s spinner to the top all day except when that reel value is 20K or 30K.</p></div></section></div>
