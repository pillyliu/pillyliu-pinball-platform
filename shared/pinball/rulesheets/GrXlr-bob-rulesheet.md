<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">The Amazing Spider-Man SS - Gottlieb | 1980</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/501bbcea-5ea1-4919-a55c-e82e4f7e870a-medium.jpg" alt="The Amazing Spider-Man backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> SM</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">start with up top via left orbit or bank shots off of the right side drop targets to advance base bonus. Shoot any lit advance bonus lane when possible. If you complete the 1-2-3 to light \#2 saucer for collect bonus, try to get it. Shatzing the lit return lanes back and forth is an option if you can do it.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">All target shots come with rebound risk. Balls can fall from the top right half of the game and center drain. The left slingshot is the more dangerous of the two.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Spiderman is bonus-heavy. Base bonus is built by lanes and targets; when lit, the A and B lanes give more than one bonus advance. The top A lane lights the right return lane and left outlane for bonus multiplier increases and raises the value of the lane to 5,000. The top B lane lights the left return lane and right outlane for the same. I try for the B on the skill shot because during normal play, balls go into the A chute on the right side more often than into the B arc on the left, so you have a better chance of getting all lanes lit if you plunge the B.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The top center stand-up target gives multiple bonus advances the first time it’s hit, advances your bonus X one level the second time it’s hit and just lights special after that. You also advance the multiplier by hitting the lit drop target in the bank on the right, plus a 10K score value. The lit target moves, so try to hit each of them when they are lit in turn. The other way to advance the multiplier is to complete the drop target 3-bank in the center.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus goes up to 29K, multiplier to 5X for 145K total. Bonus can be collected in the \#2 saucer at the top left after you’ve collected all of the 1-2-3 saucers. Wait to collect it until your bonus and multiplier are built up since the collect feature resets. Plunges and shots to the 1-2-3 area do not always land in a saucer; the ball can go around the left of saucer \#1.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Getting both A and B lights one of the drop targets on the right for an Extra Ball, if enabled. The red star rollover left of the top A lane lights the bumpers.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Both return lanes are shatzable to increase your base bonus and also your multiplier when lit. If you can do it, this strategy beats all others so long as you survive; once in a while, a shatz will rainbow-arc out the top of the return lane and go out of control. If you’re not comfortable shatzing, general strategy is UTAD, via the left orbit from the right flipper and bank shots off the right drop targets from the left. Either way, once your bonus and multiplier are maxed out, try to finish 1-2-3 and collect your bonus in the \#2 saucer.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">the “B” lane at the top.</p></section></div>
