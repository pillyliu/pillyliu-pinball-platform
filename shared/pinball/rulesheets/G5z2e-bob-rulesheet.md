<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Warlok SS - Williams | 1982</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/0c47c693-44b5-4e2f-83d5-ad78b9287358-medium.jpg" alt="Warlok backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">shoot the three \#1 drop targets, then left spinner / orbit “Big Shot” all day unless the return from it drains down the middle; in that case, get the \#3 drop targets instead and shoot the spinner next to them.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Similar to Blackout, mostly bumper feeds, a left orbit shot at an unlucky speed or bounce that center drains, and once in a while a drop target rebound. Slingshot drains are rarer than on most games.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing any set of drop targets lights the adjacent spinner: left bank the left spinner, top bank the top right one and center drop bank the mid-right side spinner. Spinner points can be significant at 2K per spin.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is earned by hitting the three drop target banks; you get up to 7K for each of the three numbered sets. Once you complete a target bank, the gate on the right lights to collect bonus, which does not reset for the rest of the ball. You do have to re-complete a numbered bank to recollect its bonus through the gate, though. Have all three sets done and you can collect all three bonuses with your multiplier.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing the top X-Y-Z lanes is your bonus multiplier advance up to 5X for a maximum of 105K. With the gate active, there’s no limit to how many times you can collect bonus. Bonus and multipliers reset each ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The return lanes and the upper left stand-up target are each worth 7K and also spot a \#3 target from the top bank. It’s pretty easy to get a ball approaching a flipper to deflect up a return lane to score it. Balls also zip fast down the return lanes and transfer easily to the opposite flipper.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">get one of the top lanes. There should be a gate right of the “Z” lane; if the gate has been removed, the ball can dribble down without hitting anything on the plunge.</p></section></div>
