<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">World Cup SS - Williams | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/71efcf05-564c-4033-99b4-9cafc9625378-medium.jpg" alt="World Cup backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> WC</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD, preferably up the left side.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Bias Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls exiting the bumper area that land on the rubbers outside the flippers or on the flipper elbows where you can’t control them. Rebounds from any shot other than up top or into one of the two saucers are risky.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">One of the first games with kickouts behind the flipper elbows, World Cup requires players to learn ball control without the ability to cradle. Bonus is the primary element here, so we’ll begin there.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is 2000 for each goal up to 18. Goals are scored by shooting the ball into either side saucer or the top center lane when these are lit (they light together). To light them, score the four buttons leading towards each midgame saucer. You also get a button advance by hitting the “ball” slingshot below the S stand-up target in the upper left. The left orbit, the top right inverted-J “corner kick” shot and the “super ball” shot at the top right just inside the J all light the goal. The corner kick directly feeds the right saucer to score a goal.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The top center lane can also score a goal on the plunge; the light alternates on/off on slingshot hits, e.g. note how it goes on and off as the ball is bouncing back and forth slowly settling down towards going through a top lane.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Goal count is carried over from ball to ball. Each goal also advances the value of either the left orbit or the corner kick J-shot from 1K to 4K on the left and 1K to 3K on the right; it starts by advancing the left orbit, then the J, and keeps alternating back and forth until both are at maximum value. Those values are also carried over.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus multipliers are earned by spelling S-T-A-R. The top lanes each give one letter at a time, the upper stand-up targets and the flipper kickouts each give one as marked. Multipliers go 2X, 3X and 5X for a maximum of 180K. Bonus multiplier is not carried over.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The outlanes and the spinner are largely irrelevant.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you hold the flippers up when the ball comes to them softly, the ball will roll backwards into the kickers, giving you the choice to flip or continue to let the ball roll into the kicker. The kickers tend to shoot the ball out in the general direction of the opposite goal saucer, although not over the buttons en route. The goal saucer kickouts should go back towards the same-side flipper, giving you a chance to set up another shot with a live catch or kicker drop-in with a hold.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy: Since goals and point values are carried over, building both early is key. Go for the left orbit shot to the top and just keep taking STAR letters and goals as long as you can. The game is really about going up top to build things and using the kickouts to collect needed STAR letters and shoot the goal saucers for you. Don’t shoot at the saucers with the flippers\! Never shoot the spinner.</p></div></section></div>
