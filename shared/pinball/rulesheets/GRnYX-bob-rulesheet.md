<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Ice-Revue EM - Gottlieb | 1965</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/ac44671b-df06-4ca3-9cb0-6300fba434d4-medium.jpg" alt="Ice-Revue backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">The only safe shots are successful saucer shots, which eject to the opposite-side flipper. Everything else has rebound drain risk.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Each top lane lights a bumper for 10 (vs. 1 point). Each stand-up target lights an outlane for 50 (vs. 5 points). Pairs of stand-up targets raise the value of the two center saucers from 50 to 100 and 200.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There are two versions of this game, one with 4 outlanes, another with 6. The 4 outlane version has small, probably weak slingshots next to the flippers. Learn to manage their kicking. For the 6 outlane version, there are no slingshots, just the outlanes and the flippers. Expect to (tilt permitting\!) do a lot of nudging as the ball rattles around the outlane divider posts - you’ll be trying to get it to walk inward to a flipper.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">It’s critical to get a different top lane each ball, since the ball might never go back up through any of them to light another bumper.</p></section></div>
