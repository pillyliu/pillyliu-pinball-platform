<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Sharpshooter SS - Game Plan | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/68b8a9f5-5a7a-4214-9fef-549ce74f8cba-medium.jpg" alt="Sharpshooter backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the saucer four times, once to the light spinner, the others to maximize your bonus multiplier. Then shoot targets all day from the right flipper, spinner from the left.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls coming from the top right down the candy cane or falling down through the spinner can hit the little posts below them and deflect into the left slingshot. All drop targets have rebound risk. Hold your right flipper up when the ball is in the bumper area to reduce the chance of a center drain. Balls falling through the middle left lane left of the targets can center drain if at the wrong speed.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There are three good ways to score here: the 50K lane, bonus and the spinner. We’ll do bonus first.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">One way to earn base bonus is the SHOOTER drop target bank. Second is the S-H-A-R-P lanes the first time you get each letter in the set. Third, the SHARP lanes each light a star rollover in the top horseshoe for bonus advances. Base bonus goes up to 29K. Bonus multiplier is raised by completing SHARP, SHOOTER or the saucer shot. Bonus multiplier goes up to 5X for 145k total. Bonus multiplier, saucer value and lit lanes and rollovers all carry over to future balls; only the drop targets reset.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Lighting 2X bonus lights the spinner on the right, your next point source. Once lit, it stays on the rest of the game. Enjoy the “galloping” sound it makes, unlike typical spinners.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The highest value shot on the game is the always-lit 50K lane on the left next to the “R” lane. It’s possible, though difficult, to shoot it from the right flipper via going up the 1000 lane below it. This will often get you two 50Ks if you make the shot since the ball often comes right back down the way it went up.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The two lower right bumpers can quick-drain you down the middle if you’re not alert. A good safety play that looks amateurish but isn’t is to simply hold the right flipper up when the ball is in the bumpers.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy is to get your bonus multiplier up and spinner lit a.s.a.p., then spinner all day from the left flipper and SHOOTER drop targets all day from the right, plus that 50K shot if you can make it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: balls coming down the 1000 lane left of the drop targets and balls that fall back down after not going all the way to the top on spinner shots.</p></div></section></div>
