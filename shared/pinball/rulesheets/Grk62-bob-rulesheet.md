<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Rock SS - Gottlieb | 1985</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/8fe12b7c-8dfd-4574-bfd4-39c4eeba17d3-medium.jpg" alt="Rock backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> ROCK</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Lit spinners all day.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls exiting the upper playfield that don’t drop cleanly into the far left lane. Rebounds off the center targets. Once in a while, a ball won’t feed cleanly from a side lane to a return lane and will rattle into an outlane. A halfway spinner shot that falls back down can go out of control.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Most players attack this game by going all spinners, although double scoring, bonus and extra balls all help, too.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The lit spinner is worth 10K per spin, and one is always lit; the slingshots change the lit spinner.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">For bonus, you get two advances for hitting the lit target among the center 5-bank (light cycles on a timer) and one advance for any of the four return lanes if lit. The return lanes go out when collected and can only be turned back on by hitting the “reset letters” bullseye stand-up target to the right of the 5-bank.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus is 10K per letter and goes up to 150K. If you complete all 15 letters during a ball in play, it awards your bonus immediately and resets the letters. Bonus multiplier is advanced by completing the top right three A-B-C lanes and goes up to 5X for 750K total. Letters and your multiplier are saved from ball to ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you shoot the loop-around shot just right of the center of the playfield, you get a brief period of double playfield scoring. This is most useful if the right spinner is lit, since the return from the loop-around will usually go through the two left return lanes to feed the left flipper, giving you a shot at the right spinner for 20K a spin.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Extra balls, normally off in competition, score 500K. Finishing your letters will light it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The upper 1-2-3-4 playfield is primarily for winning replays; it awards more points for each level completed, but not enough to compete with the spinner. When the ball is in there, shoot it anyway just because it’s safe points. Get the numbers in order to advance to the next level; the one you need next will flash. Each set of four moves you on to the next level.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feed: balls exiting the upper playfield; they might not fall smoothly to the left flipper, instead bouncing off the top of the divider below the exit of the spinner.</p></div></section></div>
