<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Attila the Hun SS - Game Plan | 1984</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/8beedace-c047-4d97-b587-0362273cb15c-medium.jpg" alt="Attila the Hun backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> ATH</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">No quickie version really; you need to get all six drop targets, all three top lanes, and three captive ball hits to maximize your scoring chances. Look at the center grid and try to shoot to complete a column [best] or row of items.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Spinner shots are safe, most else has some risk. The rebounds from captive ball and drop target shots can drain. Shoot what you need and follow the rebounds closely.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus is earned for each A-T-T-I-L-A drop target, each top lane, and three captive balls hits, to complete the 4x3 grid in the center of the game. Completing lines in the grid, e.g. A-T-T or A-I-T-H, scores more – 25K for a row, 50K for a column. Shooting the captive ball to complete T-H-E or completing the top H-U-N lanes advances your bonus multiplier.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">A good bonus on this game trumps any other playing strategy. You need to spread your shots around and kind of get everything once. It’s much more balanced shot-objective-wise than most games, even though most of the shots feed into end of ball bonus rather than during-ball scoring.</p></div></section></div>
