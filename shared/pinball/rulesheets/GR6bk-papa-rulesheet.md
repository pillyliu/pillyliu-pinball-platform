<span class="bodyTitle">Playfield Layout</span>
<P>
Described from the flippers, and going around the playfield more or less
clockwise.</P>
<DL>
<DT><a name="Flippers">Flippers</a></DT>
<DD>There are two. Normal configuration, normal placement.</DD>
<DT><a name="ExtraBallLight">Extra Ball Light</a></DT>
<DD>Between the flippers. When <a href="#ExtraBall">lit</a>, the same
player shoots again.</DD>
<DT><a name="LeftInlane">Left Inlane</a></DT>
<DD>Normal left in lane. Scores 300 and the
<a href="#BRollover">B rollover</a>. Ball feeds to the left flipper.</DD>
<DT><a name="LeftOutlane">Left Outlane</a></DT>
<DD>Normal left outlane. Scores 300 and the
<a href="#ARollover">A rollover</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="LeftSlingshot">Left Slingshot Bumper</a></DT>
<DD>Normal slingshot shape, but with no kicker. Scores 10. </DD>
<DT><a name="LKickout">Left Ball Kickout</a></DT>
<DD>A cup shaped ball trap facing up the playfield. The ball can stop here,
before being kicked back in to play. The kicker sends the ball over the
<a href="#LKickRoll">Left Ball Kickout Rollover</a>
and in to the <a href="#LSaucer">Left Ball Saucer</a>. Scores 500 points.</DD>
<DT><a name="LKickRoll">Left Ball Kickout Rollover</a></DT>
<DD>Scores 100 points and can be lit for <a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a>. Scores Extra Ball when lit.</DD>
<DT><a name="LSaucer">Left Ball Saucer</a></DT>
<DD>A standard ball saucer. Scores 3000 points, plus advances the
<a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one step (1000 points * bonus multiplier)
for each of the lit <a href="#LSpots">Left Spots</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="LSpots">Left Spots</a></DT>
<DD>Three small playfield lights. Each one corresponds to one of the three
<a href="#DropTargets">Drop Targets</a>. When the drop target is down, the
spot is lit. The <a href="#Alternate">Alternating Relay</a> changes the
lit Spots from Left to <a href="#RSpots">Right</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="BRollover">B Rollover</a></DD>
<DD>A standard rollover lane. Starts out with "B" lit. When the ball rolls
over a "B", either this one or the <a href="#LeftInlane">Left Inlane</a>,
"B" is collected and the light is turned off. Scores 300 points, and
advances the <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one step.</DD>
<DT><a name="ARollover">A Rollover</a></DD>
<DD>A standard rollover lane. Starts out with "A" lit. When the ball rolls
over an "A", either this one or the <a href="#LeftOutlane">Left</a> or
<a href="#RightOutlane">Right</a> Outlanes, "A" is collected and the light
is turned off. Scores 300 points, and advances the
<a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one step. Can be lit for <a href="#Special">Special</a>
by completing the <a href="#ABCRollover">A-B-C rollovers</a> and dropping
all three <a href="#DropTargets">Drop Targets</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="CRollover">C Rollover</a></DD>
<DD>A standard rollover lane. Starts out with "C" lit. When the ball rolls
over a "C", either this one or the <a href="#RightInlane">Right Inlane</a>,
"C" is collected and the light is turned off. Scores 300 points, and
advances the <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one step.</DD>
<DT><a name="#SpecialLight">Special Light</a></DD>
<DD>A large red playfield lamp insert. Lit by completing the
<a href="#ABCRollover">A-B-C rollovers</a>
and dropping all three <a href="#DropTargets">Drop Targets</a>. Remains lit
until the ball drains. While lit, any ball going through the
<a href="#ARollover">A Rollover</a> lane collects one
<a href="#Special">Special</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="LPop">Left Pop Bumper</a></DT>
<DD>A standard pop bumper. Scores 100. </DD>
<DT><a name="MPop">Middle Pop Bumper</a></DT>
<DD>A standard pop bumper. Scores 10. Used to fire the
<a href="#Alternate">Alternating Relay</a>, and the
<a href="#FSRelay">00-90 Unit</a> for the
<a href="#Match">Match</a> feature. </DD>
<DT><a name="RPop">Right Pop Bumper</a></DT>
<DD>A standard pop bumper. Scores 100. </DD>
<DT><a name="DropTargets">Drop Targets</a></DT>
<DD>Three drop targets with rubber posts behind them. Scores 300
points, advances the <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one step, and
lights one Spot for each drop target hit. Completing all three drop targets
lights <a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a>. Completing all three drop targets
and the <a href="#ABCRollover">A-B-C rollovers</a> lights
<a href="#Special">Special</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="Stars">Star Rollovers</a></DT>
<DD>Three star rollovers, directly below the three drop targets. Scores
10 points.</DD>
<DT><a name="RSpots">Right Spots</a></DT>
<DD>Three small playfield lights. Each one corresponds to one of the three
<a href="#DropTargets">Drop Targets</a>. When the drop target is down, the
spot is lit. The <a href="#Alternate">Alternating Relay</a> changes the
lit Spots from <a href="#LSpots">Left</a> to Right.</DD>
<DT><a name="RSaucer">Right Ball Saucer</a></DT>
<DD>A standard ball saucer. Scores 3000 points, plus advances the <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> one
step (1000 points * bonus multiplier) for each of the lit <a href="#RSpots">Right Spots</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="RKickRoll">Right Ball Kickout Rollover</a></DT>
<DD>Scores 100 points and can be lit for <a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a>. Scores Extra Ball when lit.</DD>
<DT><a name="RKickout">Right Ball Kickout</a></DT>
<DD>A cup shaped ball trap facing up the playfield. The ball can stop here,
before being kicked back in to play. The kicker sends the ball over the
<a href="#RKickRoll">Right Ball Kickout Rollover</a>
and in to the <a href="#RSaucer">Right Ball Saucer</a>.
Scores 500 points.</DD>
<DT><a name="RightSlingshot">Right Slingshot Bumper</a></DT>
<DD>Normal slingshot shape, but with no kicker. Scores 10. </DD>
<DT><a name="RightOutlane">Right Outlane</a></DT>
<DD>Normal right outlane. Scores 300 and the <a href="#ARollover">A rollover</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="RightInlane">Right Inlane</a></DT>
<DD>Normal right in lane. Scores 300 and the
<a href="#CRollover">C rollover</a>. Ball feeds to the right flipper.</DD>
<DT><a name="BonusLights">Bonus Lights</a></DT>
<DD>In the lower center of the playfield, above the flippers, and below the
<a href="#Stars">Star Rollovers</a>, are ten playfield lamps labeled 1000, 2000 ... 10,000.
Showing the player's current <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> score. There are also
"2X" and "4X" lights, showing the <a href="#BonusMultiplier">Bonus Multiplier</a>.</DD>
<DT><a name="ManualPlunger">Manual Plunger</a></DT>
<DD> This is a normal plunger.  Its lane leads to the top arch over
the <a href="#ARollover">A</a>, <a href="#BRollover">B</a>, and
<a href="#CRollover">C</a> rollover lanes.</DD>
</DL>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">Rules</span>
<P>
There are only a two main goals, completing the A, B, and C
rollovers and completing the three drop targets. There are three
secondary goals, collecting the Extra Ball, maximizing the Bonus
count, and collecting the Special.</P>
<a name="ABCRollover">
<p><span class="bodyTitle">A-B-C Rollovers</span></a>
<P>
Roll the ball over the rollover switches marked with
"<a href="#ARollover">A</a>", "<a href="#BRollover">B</a>", and
"<a href="#CRollover">C</a>" lamps. Lit letters have not been collected yet.
When complete, the end of ball <a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> is doubled
(2X for most balls, but 4X for the last ball). Completing the A-B-C
Rollovers is one of two requirements for lighting <a
href="#Special">Special</a> on the <a href="#ARollover">A
Rollover</a> lane.</P>
<p>
<a name="ExtraBall"><span class="bodyTitle">Drop Targets and Extra Ball</span></a>
<P>
Knocking down all three drop targets lights the Extra Ball
feature, but only "sometimes". There are two Extra Ball lights on the
playfield, at the <a href="#LKickRoll">Left Ball Kickout
Rollover</a>, and one at the <a href="#RKickRoll">Right Ball Kickout
Rollover</a>. Only one can be lit at a time, and the
<a href="#Alternate">Alternating Relay</a> is used to toggle between
them. An additional wrinkle is imposed though, the Extra Ball is only
lit for certain <a href="#BonusLights">Bonus Lights</a>. As the bonus
advances, if the three drop targets are all down, the Extra Ball
light will come on and go off, and will <a href="#ExtraBallSetting">cycle</a>
from side to side, so the game instruction card describes it as being
lit "occasionally". In addition to this, collecting the lit Extra Ball
from the side rollovers is not an easy shot. This limits the number
of Extra Balls collected by players. Dropping all three drop targets
is the second requirement for lighting the <a href="#Special">Special</a>.</P>

<p>
<a name="Special"><span class="bodyTitle">Special</span></a>
<P>
Once all three drop targets are down, and the A-B-C Rollovers are
complete, <a href="#Special">Special</a> lights at the <a href="#ARollover">A Rollover</a>.
It remains lit, and can be collected multiple times, until the end of
the current ball in play. There are no direct shots at the rollover
lane, though, so it is hard to collect and rare to collect more than
once.</P>
<p>
<a name="Bonus"><span class="bodyTitle">End of Ball Bonus</span></a>
<P>Assuming that the player does not Tilt the game, when the ball
drains, the current Bonus amount shown by the playfield
<a href="#BonusLights">Bonus Lights</a> will be added to the current
player's score. <a name="BonusMultiplier">If</a> the <a href="#ABCRollover">A-B-C Rollover</a>
sequence has been completed, the Bonus will be multiplied by 2. If
the game is on the last ball (ball 3 or 5, depending on <a
href="#OperatorSettings">Operator setting</a>), the Bonus will be
multiplied by 2. The maximum Bonus is 15,000 (10,000 and 5,000 lights lit),
and the maximum multiplier is 4X (2X for being on last ball, 2X for
completing A-B-C), for a grand total of 60,000 points. On a game that
rolls over at 100,000, a 60,000 point bonus is obviously significant.</P>
<p>
<a name="Alternate"><span class="bodyTitle">Alternating Relay</span></a>
<P>The <a href="#MPop">Middle Pop Bumper</a> is used to fire the
Alternating Relay. Depending on <a href="#OperatorSettings">Operator
settings</a> the game can use this to make things slightly harder
on the player. The Alternating Relay moves the <a href="#ExtraBall">
Extra Ball</a>, when lit, from side to side, and moves the lit Spots
from <a href="#LSpots">Left</a> to <a href="#RSpots">Right</a>.</P>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">Strategy</span>
<P>
Get the <a href="#DropTargets">Drop Targets</a> to light the Extra
Ball. Get the <a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a>. Then get the bonus
multiplier via the <a href="#ABCRollover">A-B-C Rollovers</a>. Then hit
the <a href="#LSaucer">Left</a> and <a href="#RSaucer">Right</a> saucers
(whichever one has the Spots currently lit) to run the
<a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> up. Note that the
<a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a> is <b>NOT</b> lit when the
<a href="#Bonus">Bonus</a> is up to 15,000, so you must collect the Extra
Ball before maximizing the Bonus. Try to get the playfield <a
href="#Special">Special</a> if possible, but since there is no way to
shoot for it directly, ignore it.</P>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle"><a name="OperatorSettings">Operator Adjustments</a></span>
<P>
Being an electromechanical game, Bronco has several adjustment plugs
that the operator can use to tune the game play to the location. The
options are:
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">Gameplay</span>
<P>
3 balls per game or 5 balls per game. When set for 3 balls per
game, the <a href="#LSpots">Spot</a> lights are lit on both sides
simultaneously and do not alternate, and there are more chances to
light <a href="#ExtraBall">Extra Ball</a>.</P>
<P> <a name="ExtraBallSetting">Extra Ball</a> : Conservative or Liberal. When set for Liberal, the
Extra Ball lights are both lit simultaneously and do not alternate
from side to side.</P>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">
<a name="Special">Special</a>
</SPAN>
<P>
Special options are Replay or Extra Ball. </P>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">Earnings</span>
<P>
<a name="FSRelay">There is a small stepper unit that counts down
from 90 to 00, stepping one time each time the <a href="#MPop">Middle Pop Bumper</a>
is hit. This is used to compare against the last two digits of the
players' scores at the end of the game. <a name="Match">End of game
Match</a> can be enabled or disabled. Match awards
"Special". It is possible to set the game for Match and
Special = Extra Ball, but in this case the game awards nothing if the
player matches. </P>
<P> Tilt Penalty = Ball in Play. </P>
<P> Pricing - how many coins and how many credits per coin(s).</P>
<p>
<span class="bodyTitle">Settings</span>
<P>
Here are the settings I use on mine:
<ul>
<li><a href="#Special">Specials</a> @ 78,000 and 90,000
<li><a href="#Special">Special</a> = Replay (credit)
<li>Match enabled
<li>5 Balls per game
<li>Extra Ball = Conservative
<li>Tilt Penalty = Ball in play
<li>Pricing = 1 quarter / 1 play, 2 quarter / 3 plays.
</ul>
I also set the game up for optional free play by adding an SPST
switch to the Credit Unit to jumper the switch that lets the game
know that there are credits on the game to be permanently closed. By
flipping the switch, the game reverts to normal use of the Credit
Unit.
</P>
