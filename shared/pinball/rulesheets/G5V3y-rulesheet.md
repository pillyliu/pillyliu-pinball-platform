<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Hi-Lo Ace EM - Bally | 1973</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/7823d603-3b30-4538-bafd-ff868b6f1156-medium.jpg" alt="Hi-Lo Ace backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD; once you’ve got all the cards, try to get the ball to come down through the lit non-special gate.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Mostly bumper exits, both center and outlane drains. Rebounds from the three red stand-up targets are risky and best avoided when not lit for 5,000. Balls going through the 7 and 8 card lanes can be dangerous if at the wrong speed; watch and learn at what speeds you’ll need to nudge.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Kind of a bass-ackwards UTAD game. Each card [lane or target] is worth 5000 the first time you hit it, then 500 thereafter. Get as many different ones as fast as you can. Once you’ve scored a card, ignore it for the rest of the game. Finishing Ace through 7 lights alternate outlanes for Special; finishing 8 through Ace lights alternate middle gate lanes for Special. When the middle gate special is lit, you want to get the NON-special gate lane for 5000. The only other ways to add meaningful points at that stage are by hitting the three risky button stand-up targets in the middle of the game, or getting the ball to go up the Ace lane; these are worth 5000 [vs. 500] if you’ve got 8-A. The stand-ups aren’t worth the risk when not lit.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">You need to “drain wisely”: on your first ball or two, out the side to collect the Jack or 6 if you don’t have them yet. Once you have A-7 complete, out the lit non-special outlane.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: out of those two gates, the 7-card and the 8-card.</p></div></section></div>
