<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">KISS SS - Bally | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/63246976-e83d-4334-b6be-4a09a9c34fd5-medium.jpg" alt="KISS backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> KISS</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Up Top through either spinner until your bonus is at least 44,000, i.e. you’ve finished one full set of four “K-I-S-S” letters and lit the 44K insert. At that point, if you haven’t already collected the A-B-C-D targets for double bonus, go ahead and do so. Then resume UTAD.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced, except Left to get Double Bonus letters</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls exiting the bumpers generate most of the non-missed-shot drains. Shots at the targets will rebound into the bumpers, which is why you save A-B-C-D for last. The winner is whomever survives bumper exits best after successful spinner shots.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The Skill Shot is a Big Deal, since the center lane, when the Yellow light is lit, opens the right-hand gate, and when the White light is lit, it spots you a full set of Kiss letters. If you get it with both lit, great\! This is another reason for going UTAD, to get the ball up top for a chance to open the gate when you don’t already have it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There’s a drop target bank on the left that scores Kiss when completed, plus four individual Kiss letter stand-up targets spread around the playfield. Ignore both of these completely. Doing anything other than shooting the ball up top through either spinner risks hitting the underside of a bumper, either directly or on a rebound, and getting drained. You’re better off getting the letters from the top five lanes, and of course ideally that center one when lit. You’ll undoubtedly get some Kiss letters from these targets anyway, just don’t bother trying to.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The only time to not go for the spinner is once your bonus is high enough, e.g. you have at least finished the letters four times to light the 40K super bonus, then you can go for the A-B-C-D stand-up bank on the right to get your double bonus. If Extra Balls are on, you can go for this bank a second time, which will earn you the EB. If EBs are off, ignore this bank, too, once you have your double bonus.</p></div></section></div>
