<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Wild Card EM - Williams | 1977</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/466ee7c2-5927-4c54-9303-995e561eb2ac-medium.jpg" alt="Wild Card backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via left orbit from right flipper and spinner from left flipper.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumper exits are the biggest risk. Most shots are safe if you don’t miss. The saucers should kick out to the opposite-side flipper.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Any switch hit turns on the “lights spinner” light in the left orbit. Make the shot once to light the spinner and it changes to “lights double bonus.” Make that to do so.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus is advanced by the three saucers, top and lower sides.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The upper right chute is 10K \+ 1 advance, 20K \+ 2 advances or 30K \+ 3 advances. Completing the Aces adds one level, completing Deuces adds the other level.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">As the quickie version states, UTAD. Don’t mess with the lower saucers or the two shots at the upper right; balls will go in the saucers and that chute on the right after the bumpers and slingshots do their thing.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">the 10K \+ advance bonus saucer at the top. This is non-trivial, requiring just the right speed to bounce off the top rubbers and in. If you’re almost right, you can nudge to help get it in.</p></section></div>
