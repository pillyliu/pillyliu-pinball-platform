<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Royal Flush Deluxe SS - Gottlieb | 1983</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/676f550a-d0fb-4093-93ae-d91cfb2d8795-medium.jpg" alt="Royal Flush Deluxe backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> RFD</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via left orbit lane (white).</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">usually balls exiting the top on the right side and or deflecting off of the slanted rubber above the saucer. Shots through either white lane (“open gate” on the right, orbit shot on the left) can drain if they hit the rubber below wrong. Shots at the lower (left hand) drop targets are riskier than the upper ones, though both can go out of control.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">As you would expect, similar to Royal Flush the EM, but with a small wrinkle. The SS version has the bonus scores 10 times higher than on the EM, but the pad targets behind the center drops have the same value as on the older game. This means that going for the center 3000 pad once the targets are finished is no longer worthwhile; it pales compared to the left half-orbit shot, which was increased by a factor of 10 along with the bonus. So now it’s left 30,000 lane all day once the drops are done, don’t even think about the center pad at all for the whole game.</p></div></section></div>
