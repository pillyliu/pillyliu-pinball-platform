While i was at it, i checked again if the rules had been posted here and still didn’t find anything.</p>
<p>If i’ve used the search wrong and the rules are on this forum, please pardon me. Just lmk and i’ll delete this post. If i should be posting this in the Rules Wiki, then, again: i apologize and i’m not trying to cause any problems or anything. <p><strong>Skillshot</strong><br>
The skillshot is a two-stage adventure:<br>
• Soft Plunge – Choose your fate by locking in a score that appears on the enchanted<br>
screen. Then, take the plunge down the rabbit hole to collect your reward. But<br>
beware! Striking a pop bumper or rollover will send you tumbling into chaos, failing<br>
your skillshot.<br>
• Super Skillshot – Only the cleverest explorers will find the hidden path. Plunge<br>
straight into the VUK within 5 seconds to earn 1 million points and unlock a 5-<br>
second journey on the mini playfield. Every target you hit adds another million<br>
points to your skillshot!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/crystal_ball.png?v=12" title=":crystal_ball:" class="emoji" alt=":crystal_ball:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> <strong>The Ultimate Quest – Unlocking WONDERS</strong><br>
To truly conquer Wonderland, you must collect all WONDERS letters. Each completed<br>
mode rewards a letter, but beware—some are cunningly hidden throughout Wonderland!<br>
• Shoot the Ramp – Qualifies a mode.<br>
• Shoot the Scoop – Begins a mode.<br>
• Rollover Under the Clock – Grants 5 extra seconds in any active mode, up to a<br>
maximum of 60 seconds.<br>
•You may change the lit mode insert with pop bumper hits.</p>
<p>�️<strong>White Rabbit</strong><br>
• Chase the ever-elusive White Rabbit by following his flashing shots! Hurry, don’t be<br>
late!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/cyclone.png?v=12" title=":cyclone:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":cyclone:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Caterpillar’s Riddles</strong><br>
• The Caterpillar has questions—shoot the blinking shots to prove if you are truly<br>
Alice.</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/tophat.png?v=12" title=":tophat:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":tophat:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Mad Hatter’s Tea Party</strong><br>
• Time is but a trickster’s game! Shoot the clock rollover to turn back time to 6<br>
because it’s always tea time!<br>
• A 2-ball multiball will start. A one time Add-a-ball is done if Clock rollover is shot<br>
• Smash the pop bumpers to break the teacups and throw some cake!<br>
• Gather enough hits to unlock Jackpot Madness! and hear the now famous<br>
words: JACKPOT ALICE JACKPOT!!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/diamonds.png?v=12" title=":diamonds:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":diamonds:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Knave of Hearts</strong><br>
• Shoot the left orbit to enter the Queen’s Throne Room.<br>
• You’ve been caught and must defend yourself in a courtroom showdown!<br>
• Magnetic Flippers Activated – Only precision will free you! Bashing the buttons will<br>
NOT grand you any favors and you will see that the shots will be more difficult. Hit 3<br>
targets and set yourself free!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/crossed_swords.png?v=12" title=":crossed_swords:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":crossed_swords:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Army of Cards</strong><br>
• The battle is upon you! Time to dethrone the Master Guard.<br>
• Shoot 7 times beneath the mini playfield to cut down the Queen’s army of playing<br>
cards.</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/crown.png?v=12" title=":crown:" class="emoji" alt=":crown:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20">** The Queen of Hearts Wizard Mode**<br>
To face the Queen herself, you must:<br>
• Collect all 7 WONDERS letters<br>
• Play and hopefully defeat the Jabberwocky Multiball<br>
• Play all 6 main Modes (don’t have to be completed)<br>
• Find the 4 suits of Wonderland<br>
Final Showdown:<br>
• Defeat 21 guards (1 on the lower playfield and 20 on the mini playfield!)<br>
• Time is fleeting, but Paint the Roses Red can tip the scales in your favor…</p>
<p>�<strong>Painting the Roses Red</strong><br>
• Shoot the U-turn on the left when not in a mode.<br>
• After 3 shots, you’ll enter the Throne Room—but the Queen is nowhere to be<br>
found!<br>
• During your 10-second mini playfield battle, each guard hit reduces your final<br>
Wizard Mode guard count (down to a minimum of 4 remaining!). Also, don’t forget<br>
to check out what’s hidden behind the captive ball’s there.</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/game_die.png?v=12" title=":game_die:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":game_die:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Side Quests &amp; Mini Challenges</strong></p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/dragon.png?v=12" title=":dragon:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":dragon:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Jabberwocky Multiball</strong><br>
• Hit left and right ramp targets to activate lock mode.<br>
• Shoot the right orbit to capture a ball in Jabberwock’s Lair.<br>
• Lock 3 balls to start the frenzied multiball!<br>
• Jackpot shots light up—collect them all for the Super Jackpot.<br>
• Miss the Super Jackpot? No worries—your progress carries over to the next<br>
multiball!<br>
• This mode rewards a WONDERS letter!</p>

• 5x U-Turn Left side (Paint the Roses red shot) (A)<br>
• 5x Left Orbit (B)<br>

• 5x Right Orbit (D)<br>

<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/field_hockey.png?v=12" title=":field_hockey:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":field_hockey:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Quick Croquet Multiball</strong><br>
• Hit the captive ball 5 times (when not in a mode) to trigger a 2-ball multiball!<br>
• Hit it again to light ramps and collect jackpots.<br>
• Complete 5 jackpot shots to win a WONDER letter!</p>
<p>�<strong>Midnight Madness</strong><br>
• When not in a Mode, shoot the clock rollover 12 times to turn back time to midnight!<br>
• Loop the main ramp to build up X scoring multipliers.<br>
• Possible hidden letter or jackpot reward!</p>
<p>�<strong>Cheshire Cat Mystery</strong><br>
• When not in a mode spell CAT to unlock Mystery Mode.<br>
• Shoot the scoop to receive a reward!<br>
• Press Left to collect OR Right to reveal a secret reward…<br>
• But be wary! You might get a Cat in a Bag (and nothing at all!)</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/cookie.png?v=12" title=":cookie:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":cookie:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Eat Me – Grow Alice Grow!</strong><br>
• Spell EATME by hitting side target banks.<br>
• When completed, shoot the left orbit to collect a 2x, 3x, or 4x multiplier.</p>
<p>�<strong>Outlane Save Feature – The Swirling Wonderland!</strong><br>
• Every game starts with 2 saves, visible by color:<br>
o Red = 5 seconds<br>
o Yellow = 10 seconds<br>
o Green (Max) = 15 seconds<br>
• Press top flipper buttons (left or right) to spin the Wonderland Disc for 5 seconds<br>
at a time.<br>
• Use wisely! Both saves at once cost 10 seconds.<br>
• To recharge: Shoot the Save Target near the captive ball to drain power from<br>
Wonderland.<br>
• Shoot the Right Orbit/Ramp to collect extra time (up to 15 seconds max).</p>
<p>�<strong>Bonus X – The Looking Glass Trick</strong><br>
• Shoot the two inlanes at the back of the playfield to earn Bonus X.<br>
• Use the mirror and flippers to steer the ball where needed!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/hole.png?v=12" title=":hole:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":hole:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Sneaky Wonderland Escape!</strong><br>
• If the ball sneaks into the VUK, you’ll gain access to the mini playfield.<br>
• Defeat a guard to lower Wizard Mode’s difficulty!<br>
• Can also be randomly activated for extra scoring!</p>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/dart.png?v=12" title=":dart:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":dart:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Primary Objective</strong><br>
To reach the final battle and defeat the Queen, you must complete the following:<br>
Required Tasks</p>
<ol>
<li>Collect all 7 WONDERS letters</li>
<li>Start (and ideally defeat) Jabberwocky Multiball</li>
<li>Start all main modes (completion not required)</li>
<li>Collect all four Suits of Wonderland<br>
There are many paths to accomplish these tasks, and the game offers multiple side-modes and<br>
optional features that add strategic variety and enhance replayability.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/sparkles.png?v=12" title=":sparkles:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":sparkles:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Ways to Earn WONDERS Letters</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Main Story Modes<br>
There are six main modes, each capable of awarding a WONDERS letter. Shoot the ramp to<br>
lit the scoop and shoot the scoop to start the main mode. Popbumper select which mode.</li>
<li>Croquet Multiball (Main Playfield Captive Ball)<br>
Hitting the captive ball on the main playfield can start Croquet Multiball, a 2-ball multiball<br>
in which you can earn a WONDERS letter.</li>
<li>Midnight Madness (The Watch)<br>
Advancing the watch to Midnight starts Midnight Madness, which awards a WONDERS<br>
letter.</li>
<li>Mini Playfield – Paint the Roses Red<br>
Using the left mini-loop, you can start Paint the Roses Red.<br>
• Hitting the mini playfield captive ball during this mode awards a WONDERS letter.</li>
<li>Mini Playfield – Sneak-In<br>
During Sneak-In, hitting the captive ball on the mini playfield also awards a WONDERS<br>
letter.</li>
<li>Collecting the Four Suits<br>
Gathering all four Suits of Wonderland starts a Suits Hurry-Up (which utilizes the left<br>
ramp) and awards a WONDERS letter.</li>
<li>Jabberwocky Multiball<br>
Starting Jabberwocky Multiball always awards a WONDERS letter —<br>
not only the first time, but also on every subsequent start</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="https://tiltforums.com/images/emoji/google/mag_right.png?v=12" title=":mag_right:" class="emoji only-emoji" alt=":mag_right:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"><strong>Additional Notes &amp; Hidden Features</strong></p>
<p>•<strong>Knave of Hearts</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A hidden Extra Ball can be earned by hitting the captive ball on the mini playfield<br>
during this mode.</li>
</ul>
<p>•<strong>Mad Hatter Mission</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Includes a Multiball phase.</li>
<li>Offers an Add-a-Ball feature for extended play and increased scoring.</li>
</ul>
<p>•<strong>Mystery Mode</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>One of the Mystery awards is a WONDERS letter. So, spell CAT, shoot the scoop,<br>
choose Mystery and Wonders may happen.</li>
</ul>
<h2><a name="p-99823-scoring-tip-maximize-your-points-1" class="anchor" href="#p-99823-scoring-tip-maximize-your-points-1"></a>�<strong>Scoring Tip – Maximize Your Points</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Before starting any mode, activate the Playfield Multiplier via hitting the EATME targets +<br>
left orbit shot. This dramatically increases scoring potential and is crucial for high-score runs.</li>
</ul>

Queen! And, don’t forget to enjoy the Wonders on your way.</p>
