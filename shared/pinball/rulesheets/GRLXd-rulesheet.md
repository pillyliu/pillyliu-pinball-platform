<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Supersonic SS - Bally | 1979</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/16b818a5-be53-46a4-8d76-6b61c4aebbfa-medium.jpg" alt="Supersonic backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Open the outlane gate by making the top center lane, then UTAD via the spinner until your bonus is near maximum. If by then you haven’t got 5X bonus, you can shoot the saucer when the spinner isn’t lit, then resume spinner.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">High; see Key Feeds</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This game is largely UTAD through the spinner from the left flipper, except for shooting the multiplier saucer if the ball won’t drop into it for you when caroming around in the bumpers. When the ball does go in the saucer, try to live catch the kick out from it to repeat the shot and get it to 5X in two or three quick flips.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">From the right flipper, tap pass to the left flipper if you can. If not, shoot the drop targets to score a few points and put the ball back up in the bumpers where it might go into the multiplier saucer or pop up one of the five top lanes. If the feed from the left “jet stream” chute is good, e.g. it lands on the right flipper and safely dead bounces to the left flipper where you’d prefer to have it, shoot that instead.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus goes up to 29K, so with 5X, your overall max bonus is 145K. In a good game, a high portion of your points will come from lit spinner shots and near-max bonuses.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing the 1-5 lanes is mildly useful, making other stuff advance bonus and scoring 25K once you’ve done it enough, but don’t worry about it. Given a choice of having the ball go through a lit \#2 to open the gate or through some other number to complete 1 through 5, take \#2 for the gate.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the four stand-up targets and five central rollover buttons.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If the spinner is really juicy, ignore shooting at the bonus multiplier saucer and just go spinner all day.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: the saucer kickout; out of the chute on the left; and shots that don’t go all the way to the top through the spinner but fall back.</p></div></section></div>
