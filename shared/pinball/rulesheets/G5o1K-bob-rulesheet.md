<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Orbit EM - Gottlieb | 1971</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/16470d62-96ff-43ef-a633-7a8ebb5d44d1-medium.jpg" alt="Orbit backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">shoot the spinner on the right and the vari-target on the left to increase bonus, then try to go through the top right gate to collect it; repeat.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumper action is the highest risk. Rebounds from the vari-target can center drain or on rare occasions hit the top of the left slingshot mount and bounce out the left outlane. Balls coming down either side lane or the spinner tend to be safe though not a sure thing.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">You may have seen its solid state younger brother, Super Orbit, in a tournament, but the original Orbit has pretty much the same rules as junior, just one less scoring digit. He’s the scoop.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The Skill Shot is to plunge the advance bonus lane, the center of the three at the top left. Just watch out, on some games the ball may then go SDTM.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy is three-fold: one is hitting the vari-target, which advances bonus and scores points; the further back you push it, the better, up to 5000 plus five advances. See where the ball rebounds after you hit this; you may be able to get the ball back to a cradle on the left flipper after your shot. Second is shooting the right side spinner, which also advances the bonus and is usually a safer shot than the vari-target when you miss. Third is towards the upper always-open gate, good for 3000 and a free plunge.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There’s also a lower gate, opened when the ball goes through the left return lane, so nudge the ball into that when you can. Shots through the upper gate will close the lower gate if it’s open.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus maxes out at 10,000 and is doubled on the last ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the 500-point lanes and stand-up targets.</p></div></section></div>
