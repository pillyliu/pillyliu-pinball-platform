<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Vulcan EM - Gottlieb | 1977</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/8a617271-3d24-441e-950a-3756c35d031a-medium.jpg" alt="Vulcan backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Up top via the left orbit until you have at least three numbers; complete the white [upper] drop target set; complete the center target bank; then left orbit all day.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Rebounds off of the drop targets going out of control; watch out for balls rolling off the center wedge (behind the drop targets) that go out the right outlane, either directly or via the top of the slingshot mount, or off of the rubber on the right side to the center drain.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Your first order of business is to fill out your bonus. The base is 19K, built by hitting drop targets. Bonus builds faster, though, if you’ve collected the five numbers first [1-4 via lanes, \#5 via a stand-up target], since each number lights one of the white drop targets to make it worth three advances instead of one. Once you have most of the numbers, start shooting the white drop targets. When you complete all five white targets, it doubles your bonus, too.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Okay, bonus built out and doubled, now what? The center green drop targets each raise the value of the saucer in the upper right by 1000; it’s worth 1000 to start, 5000 per saucer shot once you’ve completed the green targets. If you haven’t already finished them by accident, finish them now.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">At this point, just go UTAD on the left orbit. You’ll get 1000 per red star rollover that registers on the way up, since they’ll now be lit, plus points for a lane up top, and then the ball will fall into the bumpers where it might bounce into the saucer.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">any top lane, just don’t fall down the left orbit.</p></section></div>
