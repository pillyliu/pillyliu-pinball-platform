<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Little Chief EM - Williams | 1975</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/5dd9e5df-8f53-467d-8614-fb4ac1c9cc81-medium.jpg" alt="Little Chief backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD . . . BUT stop to shoot the right gate to collect bonus when it’s doubled and at least 7,000 base. Repeat UTAD and collect routine.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right, except for shooting the collect bonus gate</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Balls exiting the top on either side of the center saucer have high risk of outlane; see also Key Feeds below for details.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Generally UTAD, but with a few specifics.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The goal here is building bonus, doubling it, collecting it, repeating.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Lots of stuff advances bonus - - top lanes, left center rollover button, stand-up targets. Double bonus is earned one of two ways: shooting the center saucer [or having the ball drop into it from above]; or collecting the top center lane when lit. That lane is lit by collecting both the A and B lanes next to it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Three saucer shots, or three lit center lanes, advances the saucer to level 3, where a drain out the lit side scores an extra ball [if enabled].</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy: shoot up top until you get both A and B to light the bottom gate and the top center lane. Shoot top again to get the center lane for double bonus. Then shoot the open gate to collect bonus. Repeat.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Basically, every shot with the right flipper should be up top. From the left flipper, there are 4 choices, depending on where your bonus stands. If the base bonus is not maxed out, you can either transfer [may be risky] or shoot at the upper right “advance bonus when lit” mini-sling. If the bonus is maxed, or close, but not doubled and “light double bonus” is on, you can shoot the center saucer, but this is also risky since misses can drain. And if the bonus is doubled and at or close to maximum, your top priority is to shoot the collect bonus gate at the middle right of the machine.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you’re still building up bonus, try to get the ball to the right flipper; if your bonus is built up, try to get it to the left. Learning the feeds and using dead bounces and return lane transfers are the easiest ways to do so.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds: balls rolling down the left side over the 3 discs; balls through the upper right advance bonus lane; the kickout from the center saucer.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key Feed Caution Zone: when the ball is approaching the center saucer from above, it may encounter the post directly above the saucer. It may or may not also hit one of the two posts below the sides of the saucer. In either case, if the ball falls to the outside of the side posts, you risk a drain out that side of the machine, either directly or off the top of one of the slingshots. Nudge to avoid the ball rolling outside; if you can’t prevent that, be ready to try a nudge lower down to avoid side draining. You want the ball to go to the inside of these two lower posts and drop towards and ideally into the saucer. But don’t be complacent when the ball does go inside the posts towards the saucer; if it doesn’t settle smoothly into the saucer, it may become a center drain.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">the A lane at the top. B is okay, A is better. Why? A lights the upper right advance bonus slingshot, while B lights the lower left advance bonus stand-up target. The upper right sling is by far the safer of the two to shoot at, and the more likely to be hit accidentally as well.</p></section></div>
