Now, shoot the ball up top to try to collect either of the side &quot;X 10&quot; rollover lanes when the ball comes back down; these are worth 50000 with all four ace drops down and the 10X lit.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">soft plunge for the top center &quot;light both 10X&quot; lane; next best is for either side 50000 lane.  Do not full plunge!</p></section></div>
