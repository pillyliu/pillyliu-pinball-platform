<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Lawman EM - Gottlieb | 1971</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/5848cfd2-9191-4583-ae7e-05a6a53fc4c3-medium.jpg" alt="Lawman backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 2</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the stand-up target on the right side but outside the plinko lanes to open the gate, then shoot the ball to the top right and into the plinko lanes. If the gate is still open when the ball exits the plinkos, repeat; if not, reopen the gate. Shoot drop targets when the ball is on the right flipper.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Everything is risky. Watch the exit from the bottom right of the plinko lanes and note if there is rubber on the lowermost post or not. Stay alert if the ball exits the plinko lanes before getting to the bottom pair, too, it might go down the middle.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This is a 2-player version of Sheriff; it shares the playfield layout with Atlantis as well, but the rules for Atlantis differ from Lawman and Sheriff.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">There’s a ball save gate on the right, but not all the way down at outlane level, it’s one waterfall channel tier above that. The stand-up targets that are sequence advance on Atlantis are open and close gate targets here.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Unlike Atlantis, there’s no extra value in knocking down a pair of left side drop targets with a single shot. But since it’s still worth an extra 5000 when you finish the bank, they’re still a primary goal.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The plinko lanes are more important than on Atlantis, since a lit lane here is now the most valuable shot on the game, but you still need to be cautious when shooting for them due to drain risk out the bottom right if your gate isn’t open. If the gate *is* open, definitely shoot to the top right to enter the plinko lanes.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The advance bonus lanes at the top and in the waterfall are synchronized. One set is top left and right lanes plus R-L-R-L as you go down the waterfall and R on the lower left outlanes. Change the 1000 reel and they all flip to the others.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Each 1000 points scored advances the bonus 1000. Double bonus is on the last ball only.</p></div></section></div>
