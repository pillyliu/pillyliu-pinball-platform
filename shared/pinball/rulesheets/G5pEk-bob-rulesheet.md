<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Lost World SS - Bally | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/f8330671-5d26-4c7a-b083-b60645e5f91c-medium.jpg" alt="Lost World backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Shortname:</strong> LW</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">get all six letters, then shoot saucers all day.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Mild bias to Right until you have A and B, then Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Bumpers / bumper area exit. Rebounds off the targets have risk but are necessary, but note the slingshots often give you some of the targets for free. Halfway spinner shots can fall back and center drain. The saucer kickouts are usually safe.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Two major things here, bonus and the saucers. The simpler is the saucers - - the value goes up each time you make the shot, 1K-2K-4K-6K-8K-10K, then stays at 10K after one pause for a special. If the kickout from the saucers is good, you can just repeat them all day for lots of points.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The bonus method involves hitting all four letter stand-up targets and the two lanes, A and B, at the top. C+D lights double bonus; C+D+E+F lights triple bonus; add A and B for all six to get 5X bonus. A+B+C+D lights the extra ball captive ball, but that’s only worth shooting at if EBs are active since the rebound is risky. Lanes, the six letter targets and the spinner all advance your base bonus, to a maximum of 29K. By the time you get all six letters via shots at the targets and to the top through the spinner, you’ll probably be at least halfway there; don’t worry about your base bonus, let it happen. Your maximum overall bonus is 5X29K or 145,000.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Key feeds are the kickouts from the two saucers; you’ll need to determine whether to dead bounce, live catch, hold up a flipper or something else to get control of the ball after one, and they’ll likely be different from each side.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">make either the A or B lane. Once you’ve played a ball, if your shots to the top through the spinner usually go into one particular lane of the two, try thereafter to get the other lane on future plunges.</p></section></div>
