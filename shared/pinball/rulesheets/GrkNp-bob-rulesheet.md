<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Blue Chip EM - Williams | 1976</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/0b1aaa3a-7b03-46e6-b8c0-43e6a925785d-medium.jpg" alt="Blue Chip backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Up Top until you have 1-2-3 and some bonus, then shoot the 4 and 6 targets, then resume UTAD through the spinners.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced at first, bias to Left flipper once saucer is lit</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Feeds through the spinners that deflect off the side walls or balls through the double bonus lanes going very slowly (faster ones go to the opposite side flipper). Shooting the 4 and 6 stand-up targets is risky but necessary.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Shoot the ball up top through the spinners until you are able to collect the 1, 2 and 3 lights. This lights the top right saucer for 50,000. Then STOP shooting the top long enough to shoot the 4 and 6 targets to light the double bonus lanes: targets 1 and 4 light the left double bonus lane, 3 and 6 light the right double bonus lane. This is more important than usual: on Blue Chip, the numbers collected are held between balls, so double bonus stays on for the rest of the game, as does your lit saucer.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Now you can go back to shooting the spinners, preferably the right one to try to get the ball in the right saucer for 50K. The 2 target lights the left spinner, the 5 lights the right spinner, but the 5 is a center shot and not worth the risk; if you hit it, fine, if not, don’t worry about it.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The game has wide return lanes, so shatzing from right to left or vice versa to shoot the lit spinner is pretty safe.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Never shoot the 7 or 8 targets; they only help you light the special, likely worth nothing in tournament play.</p></div></section></div>
