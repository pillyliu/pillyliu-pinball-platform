<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Dark Rider SS - Komplett | 1985</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/a8389290-d9ce-40ee-accb-4ae10e3f1632-medium.jpg" alt="Dark Rider backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> alphanumeric</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Complete the drop target 4-bank twice to raise your bonus multiplier to 3X, then UTAD, via the left orbit and right collect bonus chute. If the drop targets are set to award 50,000 points for completing them the third time for a special, do that instead of the left orbit.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Right while building values, Left when collecting bonus.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Mostly bumper area exits that go into the slingshots or drain directly. Your primary shots, i.e. the drop targets, collect bonus lane and left orbit, all feed the bumpers, so you have a lot of surviving to do.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">This is a retheme of Bally’s 1979 Star Trek with different art for the playfield and backglass. About 150 of these conversion kits were made, so it’s fairly rare.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Base bonus goes up to 29K. Completing the drop targets scores 5K and raises your bonus multiplier to 2X, then 3X for an overall max bonus of 87K. Besides helping raise your bonus X, shots at the drops rebound towards the top saucer and might lead to getting more letters in RIDER [below]. The right side “warp speed” chute is your bonus collect lane and returns the ball to the plunger. Shoot this whenever your bonus is good.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Completing the RIDER letters scores the current value and raises it to the next value: 10K, 25K, 50K, Special. [Specials might be set to be worth 50K.] Besides the two top lanes and the lower stand-up targets, the top saucer scores the lit letter; which one is lit cycles on bumper hits. Completed and partial sets of RIDER letters are preserved from ball to ball.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The two star rollovers at the upper left and the two return lanes score and raise the top left “hyperspace” value up to 10K. Ignore this until you’ve got your bonus at 3X, then ignore the drop targets and shoot this instead.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you’re good at tight ball transfers via shaztzing or tap passing, transfer right to left to set up the chute shot, especially once you have 2X or 3X bonus.</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">get a letter in R-I-D-E-R that you need, preferably one from the saucer.</p></section></div>
