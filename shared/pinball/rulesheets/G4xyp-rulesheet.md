<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Super Straight EM - Sonic | 1977</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/9f8642ad-3cf9-404f-a0f5-71aeeafa50da-medium.jpg" alt="Super Straight backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 4</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">UTAD via the left side and upper right gate at first; UTAD through the spinner once that’s lit. Shoot Collect Bonus when worth 70k+.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Mild bias Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Mostly bumper exits. Shots at the Aces can rebound to the outlanes or otherwise go out of control. The spinner shot is safe unless shot dead center, when the bumper above it can smack the ball back down the center drain. The saucer kickout should be safe.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy – Initial: from the left flipper, shoot the upper right gate to go back up top for more bonus advances. From the right flipper, shoot the two Jack stand-up targets to light bonus collect; even if you miss, you may hit other stand-ups that will be useful. Getting both kings opens the lower right gate; the queen lights the rollovers; the tens light the bumpers.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Strategy changes:</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">When you have 70K+ bonus AND bonus Collect lit, change your right flipper shot to the Collect Bonus saucer. Maintain upper right gate shot from the left flipper.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">When you get the bonus maxed out at 100K, that lights the center spinner for 1000 per spin. From the right flipper, shoot to collect bonus at the left saucer. Change your left flipper shot to the spinner, assuming the spinner spins well. If the spinner is weak, you can either shatz the ball to the right flipper to set up a collect bonus shot or continue to shoot the upper gate. If you have the lower gate open, you can also shoot that.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">After you’ve collected bonus and it has reset to base value, go back to the initial strategy.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The inlanes on this game and similar vintage ones like Hot Tip and Mars Trek are wide enough to make shatzing relatively safe even for those players not very adept at this type of shot. In fact, this is a good game to learn this skill on: start with games like this with wide gaps between the bottom of the return lane and the bottom of the slingshot above it, then work your way into narrower ones like High Speed. Here, though, the shatz shot will not roll up the return lane due to the sharp corner in the wire, it will simply serve as a flipper transfer mechanism.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you happen to hit one of the Ace targets along the way [which often happens when you miss the gate shot on the right or the Jack shot on the left], go ahead and shoot the other Ace if you get the chance. The two aces light a return lane [it alternates] for Double Bonus. While double bonus can be very valuable, the rebound risk off the Aces tends to be high, so I don’t shoot at them unless the bonus is near maximum. You can’t get double bonus via a direct shot, only by having the ball fall into one of the return lanes. It happens, but you’re more likely to drain out the side than get double bonus.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The game has no real “key feeds,” but watch the kickout from the Collect Bonus saucer on the left.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">If you have the lower gate on the right side that returns the ball to the plunger lane open, shooting that is an alternative to shooting the upper gate.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Like its Sonic / Segasa brethren Cherry Bell, Mars Trek and Monaco, Super Straight has the “should I shoot to collect bonus with double bonus lit?” quandary, since the bonus value is not doubled when shooting the collect saucer. Check your competitive position before shooting it, since you’ll need to “refill” the bonus before you drain to make your doubler worthwhile again.</p></div></section></div>
