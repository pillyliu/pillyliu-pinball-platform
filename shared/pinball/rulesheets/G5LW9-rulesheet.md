<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Playfield Tour</SPAN>
<P>
Clockwise from the flippers:
<DL>
<DT>
BALL SAVE POST
<DD>
raised
by hitting the "Pinocchio" drop target, this post times out with the sounding
of four "gongs". Yes, you can still drain around the post, if your
flipper work is bad enough.
<DD>
"Tell a Lie" INLANE
<DD>
scores 10; when
lit, raises the Pinocchio drop target; feeds the left flipper.
<DT>
"2X Gingy" INLANE
<DD>
scores 10; when lit (alternating with the "2X Fiona"
inlane, switched by either sling), doubles the value
of the Gingy Spinner shot for a brief time; feeds the
left flipper.
<DT>
"Extra Ball" OUTLANE
<DD>
scores 10; when lit, awards an extra ball.
<DT>
"Fiona" TARGET
<DD>
scores the "Fiona" value in certain modes, along with 100K for the first
hit of each ball, increasing by 15K with each additional shot; lights
the Fairy target.
<DT>
"PUSS in Boots" TARGET (LEFT ORBIT)
<DD>
scores the "PUSS in Boots"-value in certain modes, along with 100K
for the first hit of each ball, increasing by 10K with each additional
shot. The shot does not "orbit", but returns back down the left
side to the flippers, and in most cases drops the CASTLE GUARD
target, and advances its value, and lights the Castle Guard Jackpot
for a short time.
<DT>
"Pinocchio" DROP TARGET
<DD>
scores 200K for the first hit of each ball, increasing by 25K for
each additional hit; raises the ball saver post for a short period
of time. Up at the start of each game.
<DT>
"B-A-B-Y" DROP BANK TARGETS
<DD>
scores 750 for each target dropped. When completed, reduces the
number of "DROP TARGET HITS TO START MULTIBALL" shown above the
right flipper by one; completing the count starts Shrek Multiball
(see below). The lowest target is also lit for "SKILL SHOT" at the
start of each ball; hitting it when lit scores 500K the first time
in each game, increasing by 250K each additional time the Skill
Shot is made.
<DT>
RAMP
<DD>
scores 5,510 for the first shot of each game, increasing by 500 for
each additional shot. Increases the ramp count; reaching indicated
numbers lights the upper captive ball for Extra Ball. The Castle
Guard target drops in front of this ramp; hitting it (whether or
not the ramp shot is completed) scores the sum of the point lights
flashing in front of the ramp, and/or starts Repay Your Debt mode,
if that light is flashing. The ramp also lights Merlin and advances
the pop bumper values; it feeds the lower left flipper via the "2X
Gingy" inlane.
<DT>
"SHREK" ("X-X-X") BANK TARGET
<DD>
scores 10 for each target dropped.  When completed, scores a "Shrek"
value in certain modes, and advances the end of ball bonus multiplier,
to a maximum of 5X. The first completion of each ball scores 100K,
increasing by 20K with each additional completion to a maximum of
250K.
<DT>
"Prince Charming"
<DD>
scores 10; advances toward the Prince Charming Awards (see below).
<DT>
BUMPERS
<DD>
one near Shrek (green, scores 220 per hit), one tucked under the
mini playfield (white, scores 220 per hit), and a third one directly
behind Prince Charming (yellow, scores 520 per hit). Increased in
value by successive ramp shots: the first increases the white bumper
to 520 per hit, the second increases the red bumper to 520 per hit,
and the third triggers quotes with each bumper hit. Hitting any
bumper rotates both the currently selected Prince Charming mode and
swamp mode through the remaining unplayed modes.
<DT> "Merlin" ("MYSTERY")
<DD>
scores 10; when lit, scores a "randomly" selected
value (see below).  Lit at the start of each ball; ejects into the
bumpers.
<DT>
Donkey's MINI-PINBALL
<DD>
a separate mini playfield, complete with its own mini flippers and
pinball. There are five scoring shots on the playfield (from left
to right):
<UL>
<LI> FIONA  (left orbit)
<LI> MAN "Gingy" (standup target)
<LI> SHREK  (standup target)
<LI> PUSS  (ramp, feeding left mini flipper)
<LI> CHARM  (right orbit)
</UL>
There are also inlanes on both sides of the playfield, feeding their
respective mini flippers; the mini ball is placed into play by being
shot up the right inlane.  Completing all the character shots on
the mini playfield starts Donkey Multiball. The mini playfield shots
also add points to the Jackpot value for "Far Far Away".
<DT>
"Puss in Boots JACKPOT" LANE
<DD>
when lit, the first Jackpot of each ball scores 500K, increasing
by 50K with each additional Jackpot; the Jackpot light is timed,
so a lucky bounce and a skillful shot can score two PIB Jackpots
from one lighting. When not lit, the first shot of each ball through
the lane scores 250K, increasing by 25K with each additional shot
to a maximum of 500K. The lane can also be lit for Super Jackpot,
and is also the secret Skill Shot; hitting this lane when "SKILL
SHOT" is lit scores double the current Skill Shot value, and increases
the next Skill Shot award by 250K. The lane passes under the Donkey
playfield, feeding the right orbit.
<DT>
"Donkey" TARGETS
<DD>
when lit, scores the "Donkey" value in certain modes. Between modes,
score 100K for the first hit of each ball, increasing by 10K with
each additional hit, and add 1 second to the starting time for the
next round of Donkey Pinball (which starts at 15 seconds).
<DT>
UPPER CAPTIVE BALL
<DD>
scores 11,040; adds a letter to "P-I-N-B-A-L-L".  This shot is also
lit for a Jackpot in certain modes, and for Extra Ball, and is the
second half of the "PINBALL COMBO", beginning with either orbit
shot. The Combo value starts at 500K, and increases by 50K for each
additional combo hit; a combo starting with the right orbit (Gingy)
scores the full Combo value, while a combo starting with the left
orbit (Puss in Boots) scores half the current Combo value.
<DT> 
"Fairy Godmother" TARGET
<DD>
scores 10; when lit, scores a "random" Fairy value (see below).
<DT>
SWAMP
<DD>
when not lit, scores a random number of points, between 20K and
120K. When lit (in order of priority): "MULTIBALL" starts Far Far
Away Wizard Mode, "P-I-N-B-A-L-L" starts Donkey Pinball (on the
mini playfield), and "SWAMP" starts the flashing swamp mode (or
Wizard mode).  The swamp ejects to the right flipper.
<DT>
LOWER CAPTIVE BALL
<DD>
scores 11040; adds a letter to "P-I-N-B-A-L-L".  This shot is also
lit for a Jackpot in certain modes.
<DT>
"Gingy" SPINNER (RIGHT ORBIT)
<DD>
when lit, scores the "Gingy" value in certain modes; hitting the
Spinner also advances toward lighting the "Swamp".  The first spinner
rotation of each ball scores 1K, increasing by 50 with each additional
rotation to a maximum of 5K.  Completing the right orbit shot exits
the left orbit (like the return from a Puss-in-Boots shot).
<DT>
"SPECIAL" OUTLANE
<DD>
scores 10; when lit, awards a Special.
<DT>
"2X Fiona" INLANE
<DD>
scores 10; when lit (alternating with the "2X Gingy" inlane, switched
by either sling), doubles the value of the Fiona shot for a brief
time; feeds the right flipper.
</DL>
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">"Prince Charming" Modes</SPAN>
<P>
The currently selected (flashing) Prince Charming mode is rotated
through the remaining un-played modes by hitting any of the three
bumpers.  Hitting Prince Charming the indicated number of times
(starting at 5, and increasing by 1 after each award) awards the
currently flashing value:
<DL>
<DT>
"Villians"
<DD>
scores 24,570 (12K prior to "v11.0") per villain hit so far in the
game. This mode does not appear to score any points whatsoever if
it's the last of the five Prince Charming modes collected, but it
still does.
<DT> 
"3 little Pigs"
<DD>
advances the pop bumpers to their maximum point values.
<DT>
"Un-Happy Hour"
<DD>
for 20 seconds, doubles the value of all scores.
<DT>
"Once Upon a time"
<DD>
starts a Jackpot shot at a randomly selected character, which counts
down from a starting value of 3M (increasing by 1M with each
additional "Once Upon a Time" mode) to a minimum of 750K, and then
times out. Hitting the selected character scores the current value.
<DT>
"Dragon MULTIBALL"
<DD>
2-ball multiball with a generous ball saver, with six Jackpot shots
(Fiona, Puss in Boots, Shrek, Gingy, and both captive balls).  The
first Dragon Multiball Jackpot of each game scores 250K, increasing
by 100K with each additional Jackpot, to a maximum of 750K. Each
Jackpot hit also resets the countdown timer to 10 seconds; if the
countdown timer expires, one of the already hit Jackpots is relit.
Collecting all six of the lit Jackpots at the same time turns off
the countdown timer, and lights the Castle Guard Jackpot lane for
Super Jackpot, which scores 3M, and restarts the mode. Dragon
Multiball ends when only one ball remains on the playfield.
</DL>
When all five Prince Charming Modes are completed, they reset, and
can all be awarded again.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Swamp Modes</SPAN>
<P>
The currently selected (flashing) Swamp mode is rotated through the
remaining unplayed modes by hitting any of the three bumpers. Hitting
the "Gingy" Spinner the indicated number of times (starting at 10,
and increasing by 5 after each award, to a maximum of 30) lights
the swamp to start a swamp mode. When "swamp" is lit, hitting the
scoop scores 500K and starts the currently flashing mode:
<DL>
"Bridge to Fiona" ("SHOOT SIDE SHOTS AND RAMP FOR BIG POINTS")
<DD>
for 30 seconds, each of these lit shots scores 500K, increasing by
100K with each additional shot, to a maximum of 1M per shot.
<DT>
"Dinner Theater"
<DD>
within 30 seconds, hit each of the 6 characters once to activate
their super powers. The first character hit scores 750K, increasing
by 50K for each additional character hit, up to 1M for the final
character. Hitting all 6 character shots also scores a 1M bonus,
for a total mode maximum of 6.75M.
<DT>
"Wrestling Match"
<DD>
every playfield switch made scores a "hit" on the Knight, who
randomly hits Shrek back. When one of the "life" bars under the
Shrek or the knight on the DMD reaches "KO", the mode ends; either
"Shrek WINS" or "knight WINS" is displayed, along with the total
number of points scored during the mode. There appears to be a point
bonus (value unknown) for winning the fight.
<DT>
"Potion Problems"
<DD>
for 30 seconds, "any shot" (Fiona, Puss in Boots, Shrek, Gingy, or
the ramp) scores 300K points, increasing by 50K each time the Donkey
targets are hit, to a maximum of 750K.
<DT>
"Ogres are Onions" ("SHOOT Shrek ("X-X-X" TARGET BANK), Prince Charming, Puss in Boots, AND Donkey. MORE PEOPLE SCORES MORE POINTS!")
<DD>
for 30 seconds, each of these 4 character shots score points depending
on the total number of different characters hit during the course
of the mode, as follows:
<UL>
<LI> 1 character - 250K per hit
<LI> 2 characters - 500K per hit
<LI> 3 characters - 750k per hit
<LI> 4 characters - 1M per hit
</UL>
</DL>
Press both flipper buttons together at the start of any mode to
eject the ball immediately. When all five SWAMP modes have been
completed, they all stay lit, and shooting the scoop with "SWAMP"
lit starts...
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Swamp Wizard Mode</SPAN>
<P>
Starting this mode scores 1M ("SWAMP BONUS"). The starting Jackpot for the mode is then set based on what you've previously done during the entire game to that point, as follows:
<UL>
<LI> 250K for each SWAMP Wizard start
<LI> 30K for each Bridge to Fiona shot
<LI> 50K for each Dinner Theater shot
<LI> 30K for each Wrestling Match shot
<LI> 15K for each Potion Problems shot
<LI> 30K for each Orges are Onions shot
</UL>
NOTE: beginning with "v11.0", the five Prince Charming mode shot counts are now reset to zero after each Swamp  Wizard mode.
</P><P>
("EVERY JACKPOT INCREASES MULTIPLIER")<BR>
("SHOOT Swamp SCOOP TO RELIGHT JACKPOTS")
</P><P>
When the mode starts, all Jackpots are lit, and the Jackpot value starts counting down from the starting value computed above to 250K; when it reaches 250K, a 3-second countdown starts to the end of the mode.
</P><P>
Hitting the first Jackpot shot scores 1X the current Jackpot value, increasing by 1X with each additional Jackpot shot. During the first grouping, hitting 1 Jackpot will light the scoop, increasing by 1 with each additional grouping, to a maximum of 7 Jackpot shots required to light the scoop. Each Jackpot can only be scored once in each grouping. Hitting the Puss/Castle Guard Jackpot combo (starting with both Jackpots lit) scores double the normal value for the Castle Guard Jackpot shot.
</P><P>
Hitting the lit scoop scores the current Jackpot value, times the current multiplier. The Jackpot value is then reset to its starting value, the multiplier is reset to 1X, all Jackpots are relit, and the ball ejected to start the next grouping. ("JACKPOT RESET / MULTIPLIERS RESET / JACKPOTS RELIT")
</P><P>
When SWAMP Wizard mode is completed, all the Swamp modes reset, and can all be awarded again.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Donkey PINBALL</SPAN>
<P>
When "P-I-N-B-A-L-L" is lit, hitting the scoop deadens the flippers on the main playfield, and activates the mini flippers on the Donkey playfield; the mini ball is launched up the right inlane to start the mode. While the "Donkey TIME" on the DMD counts down, the orange
light between the mini flippers remains lit, and any drain is relaunched; the mode ends when the mini ball drains with no time remaining.
</P><P>
The first shot at each character of each Donkey Pinball session scores the character's base value (100K for Fiona, 50K for all others), increasing by the base value with each additional shot.
</P><P>
The first character completed (3 shots for Man, 4 for Puss, 5 for all others) of each session scores 500K and increases the playfield multiplier for the rest of the ball in play, increasing by 500K for each additional completion to a maximum of 1.5M and 3X. All character
shots score half their base value (times any playfield multiplier) after the character has been completed. The playfield multiplier resets at the start of each Donkey Pinball session.
</P><P>
Completing all five characters reactivates the main playfield flippers, and starts...
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Donkey MULTIBALL</SPAN>
<P>
Press both flipper buttons together at the start of this mode to bypass the lengthy DMD instructions:
</P><P>
("SHOOT CHARACTER SHOTS FOR JACKPOTS")<BR>
("CHARACTER JACKPOTS ALTERNATE PLAYFIELDS")<BR>
("COLLECT EACH CHARACTER 2 TIMES TO LIGHT SUPER JACKPOT")<BR>
("LIT CAPTIVE BALL SPELLS P-I-N-B-A-L-L")<BR>
("SPELL P-I-N-B-A-L-L TO ADD A BALL")<BR>
("Donkey TARGETS RECATIVATE MINI PLAYFIELD")<BR>
("LIGHT ALL CHARACTERS FOR SUPER JACKPOT")
</P><P>
Each character needs to be completed both on the mini playfield and on the main playfield (where all balls are launched, with a generous ball saver). The lights in front of each character on the main playfield and on the mini playfield are lit when that shot is
qualified; the character lights above the left flipper are flashing when one of the shots for that character has been made, and on steady when both shots have been made.
</P><P>
Each Jackpot scores 500K, plus a 100K bonus for each character already completed (?). Completing all characters lights the Castle Guard Jackpot lane for Super Jackpot. ("SHOOT SUPER JACKPOT FOR
2,000,000") When the Super Jackpot is hit, all Jackpots reset; completing them all and hitting the Super Jackpot a second time in the same Donkey Multiball scores 3M. Scoring the Super Jackpot also lights Merlin for a few seconds; when lit, Merlin scores a "TRIPLE SUPER SECRET JACKPOT", worth triple the value of the Super Jackpot just awarded.
</P><P>
The mini ball is relaunched whenever the orange light between the mini flippers is lit. When time expires, hitting either of the Donkey targets on the main playfield restarts the clock ("Donkey TARGETS
RELAUNCH MINI-BALL").
</P><P>
Donkey Multiball ends when only one ball remains on the main playfield; there is a good "grace period" when only one ball remains in which to score additional Jackpots, or add additional balls to continue the mode.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">End of Ball Bonus</SPAN>
<P>
The end of ball bonus starts at 75K on the first ball, increasing by 25K on each additional ball. Added to the bonus are the following values per character shot made during the ball:
<UL>
<LI> Donkey - 20K	
<LI> Man 	- 30K
<LI> Charm 	- 20K
<LI> Puss 	- 5K
<LI> Fiona 	- 10K
<LI> Shrek 	- 10K
</UL>
This total is then multiplied by the bonus multiplier accumulated during the ball to get the bonus total.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Fairy Godmother AWARDS</SPAN>
<P>
Hitting the lit Fairy Godmother target awards one of the following:
<UL>
<LI> 3 Charming HITS
<LI> ADD-A-BALL (only awarded while a multiball mode is in progress)
<LI> Repay Your Debt Time (only awarded while Re-pay Your Debt is in progress)
<LI> Un-HAPPY HOUR TIME (only awarded while Un-Happy Hour is in progress)
<LI> HURRY UP RESET (only awarded while .Once upon a Time. is in progress)
<LI> PINBALL LETTER (only awarded when P-I-N-B-A-L-L is not already lit)
<LI> MORE SWAMP TIME (only awarded while a Swamp mode is in progress)
</UL>
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Merlin</SPAN>
<P>
Hitting the lit Merlin awards one of the following:
<UL>
<LI>  	1 MILLION ("BIG POINTS")
<LI>  	2 MILLION ("BIGGER POINTS")
<LI>  	BONUS MULTIPLIER (increases by 1X)
<LI> 	COLLECT BONUS (just like at the end of a ball)
<LI>  	Castle Guard JACKPOT LIT (staying lit until you hit it)
<LI>  	"Baby" MULTIBALL (awarded more often when needed for "Far Far Away")
<LI>  	HOLD BONUS X (holds the bonus multiplier until next ball)
<LI>  	LIGHT EXTRA BALL
<LI>  	LIGHT SPECIAL (on the left outlane)
<LI>  	LIGHT PINBALL (on the scoop)
<LI>  	LIGHT SPECIAL (on the right outlane)
<LI>  	LIGHT Swamp MODE (on the scoop)
<LI>  	POPS MAXED
<LI>  	RAISE BALL SAVER (between the flippers)
<LI>  	RAISE Pinocchio target
<LI>  	SPOT Character (completes one character for Donkey Pinball)
</UL>
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">START Far Far Away</SPAN>
<P>
In a refreshing improvement over previous Stern/PLD games, none of these awards will be given when they would be useless (for example, "LIGHT PINBALL" when it's already lit). One exception: you can collect "LIGHT Swamp MODE" if the Swamp mode is already lit but not showing, hidden behind the higher-priority "PINBALL" being lit on the scoop.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Baby MULTIBALL</SPAN>
<P>
To start Baby Multiball, complete the Baby targets 3 times, increasing by 1 for each additional Baby Multiball start, to a maximum of 6; the first Baby Multiball (with a very short ball saver) starts with 2 balls, with each additional Baby Multiball starting
with 3 balls. The value of each Baby Multiball Jackpot is 20K times the number of switch hits required to score the jackpot, as follows:
</P>
<TABLE border=1>
<TR>
<TD> BMB session  </TD><TD>  1st   </TD><TD> 2nd   </TD><TD> 3rd & on</TD>
</TR>

<TR><TD> 2nd Jackpot</TD><TD>     12</TD><TD>     14</TD><TD>     18</TD></TR>

<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Re-Pay your Debt</SPAN>
<P>
For 20 seconds, lit shots score 200K times the number of Castle Guard shots made (at the front of the ramp) since the last time the .Re-pay your Debt. mode was started, to a maximum of 1M per shot.
</P><P>
If Re-Pay your Debt mode is started as an award from Merlin, lit shots score 400K each.
</P>
<SPAN CLASS="bodyTitle">Far Far Away</SPAN>
<P>
The final wizard mode, qualified by excelling in six of the other facets of the game. To see where you stand, hold either flipper for "INSTANT INFO"; the third screen shown will be the "Far Far Away" REQUIREMENTS, with those you have completed highlighted:
<UL>
<LI> "5 CG JPS" - 5 Castle Guard Jackpots
<LI> "10 Re-debt SHOTS"-10 lit shots during repay your
   debt mode
<LI> "15 Baby JPS" - 15 Jackpots during Baby Multiball
<LI> "Prince Charming" - all five Charming awards
<LI> "Donkey SJP" - Super Jackpot during Donkey Multiball.
<LI> "Swamp WIZARD 25M" - 25M or more points scored
   during Swamp Wizard mode
</UL>
Completing all of these requirements lights "MULTIBALL" on the scoop; shooting the scoop starts "Far Far Away".
</P><P>
In the first phase, played on the Donkey playfield for 18 seconds, each character shot adds 50K (Shrek and Man), 75K (Fiona and Charm) or 100K (Puss) to the starting Jackpot value of 1M ("BUILD JACKPOT").
Drained balls are re-launched as long as there is time remaining; when time expires, the mini flippers are deadened immediately to end the phase.
</P><P>
In the second phase, played on the main playfield, all balls are launched, with a generous ball saver. Each of the lit Jackpot shots score the value earned during the first phase.
</P><P>
When the ball saver has expired and only two balls remain, Merlin is lit. ("SHOOT Merlin!") Shooting Merlin scores 2M, and starts a 10-second timer for the scoop shot ("SHOOT THE SWAMP!!"). If the scoop is not hit in time, the ball in Merlin is ejected; making the shot scores 2M ("Swamp LOCK"), and Re-starts the first phase of "Far Far Away" by launching the mini ball on the Donkey playfield for another 18 seconds to build onto the existing Jackpot value. When that timer expires, the two locked balls are released to restart the second phase, and a third ball is
launched, with ball saver restarted. As before, the saucer lights when only two balls remain.
</P><P>
"Far Far Away" ends when only one ball remains on the main playfield.  At that point, all of the Far Far Away requirements are reset, and can all be earned again.
