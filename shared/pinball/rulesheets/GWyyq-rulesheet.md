<p><em>Are your skills sharpened for battle?</em></p>
<p><em>Legends of Valhalla, designed by Riot Pinball, is the 4th American Pinball title. Legends of Valhalla is an immersive pinball machine which brings the player into the world of the Norse gods and other Viking mythology. The game is a standard body design, utilizing three flippers offering a variety of fast and unique shots, including 3 metal ramps. The game rules have a variety of objectives that the player must either start or complete as they work their way through the game attempting to not only reach the <a href="https://www.thisweekinpinball.com/glossary/wizard-mode/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">wizard mode</a> Valhalla, the majestic hall of dead Vikings, but also advance to the final epic battle known as Ragnarok, the super wizard mode.</em></p>
<p><em>Legends of Valhalla has 16 intricate modes programmed in the form of Viking legends for players to explore along with many multiballs and several mini features that are unlocked during specific actions while playing the game.</em></p>
<p><em>The game also exhibits a couple of fun playfield features such as the Kraken, which grabs the ball via a hidden magnet mounted underneath the playfield and flings it around in chaos. Also featured is a Viking ship where the player builds their army by locking balls inside to begin multiball play. Additionally, Thor’s hammer, which is mounted on the back panel and activated by a 3rd flipper button, comes crashing down onto the ball which is held by a magnet just over the top lanes, simulating a dramatic lightning strike, adding to the gameplay experience.</em></p>

<li>Release Date: October 2021</li>
</ul>
<p>The <strong>Classic Edition</strong> includes black powder-coated rails, legs, hinges, lockdown bar, over 100 RGB lights, interactive Thor’s Hammer, hundreds of callouts, 22 total game modes including various Multiball modes.</p>
<p>The <strong>Deluxe Edition</strong> is limited to 500 units and includes enhanced features such as a mirrored backglass, topper, upgraded powder coated side rails, backbox hinges, lockdown bar and legs, shaker motor, sculpted and hand-painted figures, and anti-glare “Magic” Playfield Glass.</p>

<p>Non-Legend battles:</p>
<h2><a name="war-on-land-1" class="anchor" href="#war-on-land-1"></a><strong>WAR ON LAND</strong></h2>
<p>‘JOTNAR’: Shoot the RAMPS<br>
‘TROLLS’: Shoot the LOOPS<br>
‘DWARVES’: Shoot the SPINNER</p>
<p>Hit Berzerker to add 10 seconds to the timer.<br>
War on Land can be played unlimited times during the game. After all 3 characters are battled, the 4th and subsequent character is random and adds increasing value to each war on land. Each completed shot increases the base value.<br>
JOTNAR = *base scoring + (Battles played * 50k) + (Legends defeated * 100k) + 25k increment<br>
TROLLS = *base scoring + (Battles played * 50k) + (Legends defeated * 100k) + 25k increment<br>
DWARVES = * base scoring + (Battles played * 5k) + (Legends defeated * 10k) + 25k increment</p>

<p><strong>RAGE</strong><br>
To qualify: 2 completions of blood/rage targets lights shields of the gods kickbacks,<br>
save a ball and get 1 step to rage qualify and add blood to the axe onscreen.<br>
(progress carry over ball to ball)<br>
enable shield of the gods again and save a ball and get another step to rage qualify and more blood to the axe. Do this a total of 4 times to qualify rage and fill your axe with blood.<br>
Rage starts with a 6 ball multiball frenzy<br>
there are 4 villages to destroy. 6 shots per village<br>
complete all 6 shots and get add-a-ball and another village.<br>
complete all villages to end RAGE or drain.<br>
teal/green/yellow/purple arrows for villages 1-4<br>

<p><strong>WAR OF THE CLANS</strong><br>
Hit the blood/rage target 4 times to start this hurry up.<br>
Random award is generated at start: Points, Weapons or Warriors<br>
Shoot the random green shots to score the award. you have 10 seconds to hit as many shots as you can<br>
Hit the berzerker during the hurry up to get different shots (3-4 shots each set)</p>
<p><strong>RAMPAGE</strong><br>
Spell Rampage by hitting the center ramp (R-A-M-P-A-G-E) outside of any other running mode.<br>
Timer starts, all shots are lit.<br>
Hitting a shot awards 1X for the shot and restarts the timer. Hitting another shot awards 1X for that shot and restarts the timer. If you hit the same shot twice, award 2X the value and restart the timer. If you hit the same shot three times, award 3X the value, restart the timer and extinguish the shot.<br>
After a number of shots are completed the Berzerker target will pop up. Hit the Berzerker to rest all shots to 1x. Each completed shot increases the base value.<br>
RAMPAGE can be called an unlimited time during the game and you continue shooting until you miss a shot and the timer runs out.<br>
Rampage base value grows with the 22.07.22 and later code.</p>
<p><strong>ESCAPE THE KRAKEN</strong><br>
Qualify: 10 loops under the ship (progress carry over ball to ball)<br>
Mode start at 25 million and counts down with 12 second timer.<br>
Add time by hitting the right side of the mini loop<br>
Increase the dropping points by hitting the left side of the mini loop<br>
Score the value by hitting the under ship target to escape the kraken</p>
<p><strong>BERZERKER ATTACK</strong><br>
Enable in service menu Features\Berzerker.<br>
- adjust difficulty level in service menu Features\Berzerker Defenses for Extra Ball’ (3-8)<br>
- adjust Maximum extra balls (default 1 range 0-6)<br>
- when enabled berzerker rises during the 16 legend battles at a random time<br>
- you have 15 seconds to hit the berzerker when visible to add a berzerker hit<br>
- score Berzerker Attack hits during battles to award an EXTRA BALL<br>
- Berzerker attack is reset after every Berzerker Attack extra ball so multiple EXTRA BALLS are possible in a single game<br>
- Instant Info - Berzerker attack hits remaining and extra ball stats added<br>
- Berzerker attack timer is paused during war at sea/viking mb startup and scoop hits</p>

<p><strong>DOUBLE SCORING</strong><br>
Hit the right ramp 4 times during gameplay to start a 45 second double scoring. The right ramp arrow turns yellow when you need 1 more right ramp to start Double Scoring. The ship LED turns YELLOW during Double Scoring and RED the last 5 seconds of Double Scoring. Double Scoring is enabled for all modes, including wizard modes.</p>
<p><strong>MYSTERY AWARDS</strong><br>
Hit the Berzerker drop target enough times to light the scoop and right shooter lane for Mystery Awards.<br>
Awards such as 7.5 million, 10 million points, light ship locks, Add Raiders, Add Weapons, Spot a Task, Special, etc.<br>
(Mystery Awards can be set to fixed in service menu for tournaments)</p>
<p><strong>BATTLE HELPERS</strong> - THORS HAMMER and VALKYRIES<br>
Get Thors Hammer by hitting the spinner 100 times. When obtained, press the action button<br>
to defeat a legend* during battle or score points outside of a battle.<br>
(*except THOR who can’t be defeated with the hammer)</p>
<p>Summon a Valkyrie by: performing 5 combos or getting it as a mystery award<br>
Use a Valkyrie during a battle to score the next shot** to get one shot closer to defeating<br>
(**except Dearg Due who is defeated with a Valkyrie)</p>
<p><strong>TASKS</strong>: Complete 8 tasks to qualify Valhalla wizard mode<br>
WEAPONS - collect 10 weapons<br>
ARMY - collect 10 raiders<br>
COMBOS - perform 10 combos<br>
WAR ON LAND - start 1 war on land<br>
WAR AT SEA - start 1 sea battle<br>
GOD OF THUNDER - complete 2 sets of god of thunder rollovers<br>
LEGEND DESTROYER - play 4 battles (lights extra ball)<br>
CONQUEST - collect 8 qualities of a Viking (awards 25 million)</p>
<ul>
<li>FIDELITY -  1 RIGHT RAMP</li>
<li>SELF-RELIANCE - 3 BERZERKERS</li>
<li>DISCIPLINE - 25 SPINS</li>
<li>HONOR - 10 CENTER RAMPS</li>
<li>PERSERVERENCE - BLOOD AND RAGE (TARGET HIT TWICE)</li>
<li>COURAGE - 10 LOOPS (right loop to left loop or vice versa 10 times)</li>
<li>HOSPITALITY - 5 UNDER SHIP TARGET HITS</li>
<li>TRUTH - 10 LEFT RAMPS</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Wizard Modes:</strong><br>
VALHALLA: Single ball play, 60 second ball save<br>
qualify by completing all 8 tasks. shoot scoop or shooter lane to begin.<br>
you must collect all 9 weapons to deliver a final blow at the center ramp in order to defeat ODIN<br>
Shoot left ramp to light a weapon, shoot the weapon shot to collect it. A second visit to Valhalla (with increased task difficulty) is possible if you do not defeat ODIN the first time.</p>
<p>RAGNAROK - 6 ball play. 60 second ball save<br>
Play all 16 legend battles to qualify Ragnarok wizard mode<br>
3 levels for this wizard mode. 19 shots/level<br>
every shot in the game is lit a level color (purple/aqua/red)<br>
shoot all 19 shots for the next level<br>
complete all levels to complete Ragnarok or drain to end it.</p>
