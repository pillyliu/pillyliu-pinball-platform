<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Neptune EM - Gottlieb | 1978</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/d0bd3668-b531-4144-bb58-8cb557b00824-medium.jpg" alt="Neptune backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 1</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">start with UTAD to complete either all 6 red cards or all 6 black cards, then shoot for the lit “wow” targets.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Balanced</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">Feeds from the bumpers most of all, plus rebounds from the stand-up targets. When the ball goes through the left side (black) Jack lane, it will hit the slingshot and go out of control.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The goal is to collect all the cards, 6 red and 6 black. All but the black queen can be collected from lanes, although the black ace lane is the right outlane. The top and bottom saucers also award a random card of the color indicated.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The game is pretty much UTAD and nudging to get a complete set of one or both card colors. Once you have a set of either color, one card of that color will light for “wow,” worth 50,000. That’s where all the points are on this game.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The top left chute is worth a random award of 5k, 10k or 15k when unlit, but ten times one of those when lit. If you’ve finished a color and can’t directly shoot for a lit card for a wow, try to get the ball into the chute\!</p></div></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Skill Shot:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">get a card you need; try for the 10 on ball 1. If you already have all of the red cards, get the center saucer if it’s lit to award a black card. If you have all the red cards and the saucer is lit on red, go for the 10 at the middle left to try to get the ball to bounce into the top left kicker.</p></section></div>
