The Official Guide to Exploit Rescue 911
========================================

1.  Start a multiball.

2.  Shoot the Helicopter left shot thingy to collect the first jackpot.

3.  Get that ball onto the left flipper.

4.  Now the Helicopter will drop the ball into that hole which returns
the ball to the left flipper.  Shoot the other ball up the ramp and hold
up both flippers.  The upper ball will be caught on the upper right
flipper, and the ball from the Helicopter will be caught on the lower
left.

5.  Shoot the jackpot from the upper right flipper.

6.  As this ball is coming down the habitrail, shoot the ramp with the
other ball.  You should see where this is leading.

ALTERNATIVE:  If the first ball winds up on the lower RIGHT flipper,
then just always hold up that flipper and work with just one ball for
the jackpots.  This is still feasible because of the 2-stage action on
the upper right flipper.
