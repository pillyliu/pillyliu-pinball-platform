<div class="max-w-3xl mx-auto p-8 print:p-4"><h1 class="text-2xl font-bold text-dark-green mb-4">Aladdin&#x27;s Castle EM - Bally | 1976</h1><div class="flex gap-6 mb-6"><div class="flex-shrink-0"><img src="https://img.opdb.org/0e3ccf84-7c39-4ca1-a6fa-60a436646288-medium.jpg" alt="Aladdin&#x27;s Castle backglass" class="w-80 h-auto"/></div><div class="flex-1"><p class="mb-1"><strong>Players:</strong> 2</p><p class="mb-1"><strong>Display:</strong> reels</p><div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-gray-100 rounded"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Quickie Version:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Spinner all day from the left flipper, Aladdin’s Alley horseshoe shot all day from the right flipper.</p></div></div></div><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Go-to Flipper:</h2><p>Left</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Playfield Risk:</h2><p class="whitespace-pre-line">mostly bumper exits, though occasionally a clockwise Aladdin’s Alley shot will go down the middle.</p></section><section class="mb-4"><h2 class="text-lg font-bold text-dark-green mb-1">Full Rules:</h2><div class="space-y-3"><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">*Shot to master: Spinner*</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">For the Skill Shot, I prefer the C lane since the bumper below it may kick the ball back up through the lane and give you a chance to nudge it over into the D lane, giving you double bonus right away. The A lane is the riskiest, since balls through it graze the rubber below and go through the gap between the bumpers, sometimes too close to the right outlane.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The top A, B, C and D lanes each light star rollovers above and below for advance bonus and 1K. A+B lanes lights the spinner on the right and light the outlanes to collect the horseshoe value. C+D lanes light double bonus. All four lanes lights extra ball, if enabled.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">The Aladdin’s Alley horseshoe shot on the left side scores 500 to start; the value increases to 1K after the first shot and by 1K per shot thereafter until it reaches 5K. The key feed on this game is how the ball comes out of the horseshoe [how it comes down from the top B-C-D lanes and bumpers is pretty random]. You’ll usually be making the horseshoe clockwise [outside looping to inside], and it should come towards the left flipper, but if it tends to hit the left slingshot or go towards the center drain instead, use caution with this shot.</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Bonus maxes out at 15,000. [When the bonus is over 10,000, one of the 1000 through 5000 lights will be on in addition to the 10,000 light.]</p><p class="whitespace-pre-line capitalize-first-letter">Ignore the “300 plus advance bonus” targets - - this is another UTAD game, i.e. shoot the spinner from the left flipper, shoot the Aladdin’s Alley horseshoe from the right flipper.</p></div></section></div>
